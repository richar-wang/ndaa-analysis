SEC. 1051.  MODIFICATION OF ANNUAL REPORT ON UNFUNDED PRIORITIES.    Section 222a of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended — (1)  in subsection (c)— (A)  in paragraph (1)— (i)  in subparagraph (A), by  striking  “ to be achieved ” and  inserting  “ outlined in the national defense strategy required under section 113(g) of this title and the National Military Strategy required under section 139(b) of this title to be advanced ”; and 
 (ii)  by  adding  at the end the following new subparagraph: “(D)  Assessment. A detailed assessment of each specific risk that would be reduced in executing the national defense strategy required under section 113(g) of this title and the National Military Strategy required under section 139(b) of this title if such priority is funded (whether in whole or in part).”
