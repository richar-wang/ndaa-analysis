SEC. 11103.  AUTHORIZATION FOR CERTAIN PROGRAMS AND SERVICES.   Of the amounts authorized to be appropriated under  section 4902(1)(A) of title 14, United States Code , there are authorized to the Commandant for each of fiscal years 2022 and 2023— (1)  $25,000,000 for the child care subsidy program as established under section 11401and any additional eligible uses established by the Commandant under the amendment made by subsection (c) of section 11401; 
 (2)  $1,300,000 for expansion of behavioral health services in the Coast Guard under section 11412; 
 (3)  $3,000,000 for the Aqua Alert Notification System pilot program established under section 11207; and 
 (4)  $1,000,000 to prepare the evaluation of requirements for the Arctic Security Cutter.
