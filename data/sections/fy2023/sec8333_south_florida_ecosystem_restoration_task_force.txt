SEC. 8333.  SOUTH FLORIDA ECOSYSTEM RESTORATION TASK FORCE.   Section 528(f)(1)(J) of the Water Resources Development Act of 1996 ( 110 Stat. 3771 )  is amended  by  striking  “ 2 representatives of the State of Florida, ” and  inserting  “ 3 representatives of the State of Florida, including at least 1 representative of the Florida Department of Environmental Protection and 1 representative of the Florida Fish and Wildlife Conservation Commission, ”.
