SEC. 814.  CLARIFICATION TO FIXED-PRICE INCENTIVE CONTRACT REFERENCES. (a)  Authority to Acquire Innovative Commercial Products and Commercial Services Using General Solicitation Competitive Procedures .— Section 3458(c)(2) of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended  by  striking  “ fixed-price incentive fee contracts ” and  inserting  “ fixed-price incentive contracts ”. 
 (b)  Contractor Incentives to Achieve Savings and Improve Mission Performance .— Section 832 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2017 ( Public Law 114–328 ;  10 U.S.C. 1746 note )  is amended  by  striking  “ fixed-price incentive fee contracts ” and  inserting  “ fixed-price incentive contracts ”.
