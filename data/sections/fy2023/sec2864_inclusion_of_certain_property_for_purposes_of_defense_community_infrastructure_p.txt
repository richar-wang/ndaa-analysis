SEC. 2864.  INCLUSION OF CERTAIN PROPERTY FOR PURPOSES OF DEFENSE COMMUNITY INFRASTRUCTURE PILOT PROGRAM.    Section 2391(e)(4)(A)(i) of title 10, United States Code , as amended by this Act, is further amended by  inserting  “ or on property under the jurisdiction of a Secretary of a military department that is subject to a real estate agreement (including a lease or easement) ” after “ installation ”.
