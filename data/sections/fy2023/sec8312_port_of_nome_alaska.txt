SEC. 8312.  PORT OF NOME, ALASKA. (a)  In General .— The Secretary shall carry out the project for navigation, Port of Nome, Alaska, authorized by section 401(1) of the Water Resources Development Act of 2020 ( 134 Stat. 2733 ). 
 (b)  Cost Share .— (1)  In general .— The non-Federal interest for the project described in subsection (a) shall pay 10 percent of the costs associated with the general navigation features of the project during the period of construction. 
 (2)  Exception .— Section 101(a)(2) of the Water Resources Development Act of 1986 ( 33 U.S.C. 2211(a)(2) ) shall not apply to the project described in subsection (a). 136 STAT. 3783
