SEC. 8371.  PUGET SOUND NEARSHORE ECOSYSTEM RESTORATION, WASHINGTON.   In carrying out the project for ecosystem restoration, Puget Sound, Washington, authorized by section 1401(4) of the Water Resources Development Act of 2016 ( 130 Stat. 1713 ), the Secretary shall consider the removal and replacement of the Highway 101 causeway and bridges at the Duckabush River Estuary site to be a project feature the costs of which are shared as construction. 136 STAT. 3807
