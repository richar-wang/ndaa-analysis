SEC. 3118.  MODIFICATION OF COST BASELINES FOR CERTAIN PROJECTS.   Section 4713(a) of the Atomic Energy Defense Act ( 50 U.S.C. 2753(a) )  is amended — (1)  in paragraph (2)(D), by  striking  “ $750,000,000 ” and  inserting  “ $800,000,000 ”; 
 (2)  in paragraph (3)(A)(i), by  striking  “ $50,000,000 ” and  inserting  “ $65,000,000 ”; and 
 (3)  in paragraph (4)(A)(i), by  striking  “ $50,000,000 ” and  inserting  “ $65,000,000 ”.
