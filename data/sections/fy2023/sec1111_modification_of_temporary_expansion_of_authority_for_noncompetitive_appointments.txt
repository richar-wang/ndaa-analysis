SEC. 1111.  MODIFICATION OF TEMPORARY EXPANSION OF AUTHORITY FOR NONCOMPETITIVE APPOINTMENTS OF MILITARY SPOUSES BY FEDERAL AGENCIES. (a)  Extension of Sunset .— Subsection (e) of section 573 of the John S. McCain National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2019 ( Public Law 115–232 ;  5 U.S.C. 3330d note )  is amended , in the matter preceding paragraph (1), by  striking  “ the date that is 5 years after the date of the enactment of this Act ” and  inserting  “ December 31, 2028 ”. 
 (b)  Repeal of Opm Limitation and Reports .— Subsection (d) of such section is  repealed .
