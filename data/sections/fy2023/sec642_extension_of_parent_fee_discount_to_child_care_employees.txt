SEC. 642.  EXTENSION OF PARENT FEE DISCOUNT TO CHILD CARE EMPLOYEES.    Section 1793 of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended  by  adding  at the end the following new subsection: “(d)  Child Care Employee Discount .— The Secretary of Defense may, to support recruitment and retention initiatives, charge a child care employee, whose child attends a military child development center, a reduced fee for such attendance.”
