SEC. 231.  MODIFICATION TO ANNUAL REPORTS OF THE DIRECTOR OF OPERATIONAL TEST AND EVALUATION.    Section 139(h)(3) of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended — (1)  by  inserting  “ or controlled unclassified ” after “ classified ”; and 
 (2)  by  striking  “ submit an unclassified version of the report to Congress ” and  inserting  “ submit to Congress a version of the report that is unclassified and does not require safeguarding or dissemination controls ”.
