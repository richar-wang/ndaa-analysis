SEC. 581.  CLARIFICATION OF PROCEDURE FOR BOARDS FOR THE CORRECTION OF MILITARY RECORDS TO REVIEW DETERMINATIONS REGARDING CERTAIN DECORATIONS.    Section 1552 of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended — (1)  by  redesignating  subsection (j) as subsection (k); and 
 (2)  by  inserting , after subsection (i), the following new subsection: “(j)  Recommenda- tions. For a recommendation to award or upgrade a military decoration or award submitted pursuant to section 1130 of this title, a board determination in favor of the claimant shall allow such a recommendation to proceed, and an award or upgrade to be made by the applicable award authority, without regard to the statutory time limitation contained in section 7274, section 8298, or section 9274 of this title, as the case may be.”
