SEC. 9104.  REWARDS FOR JUSTICE.   Section 36(b) of the State Department Basic Authorities Act of 1956 ( 22 U.S.C. 2708(b) )  is amended — (1)  in paragraph (4), by  striking  “ or (10); ” and  inserting  “ (10), or (14); ”; 
 (2)  in paragraph (12), by  striking  “ or ” at the end; 
 (3)  in paragraph (13), by  striking  the period at the end and  inserting  “ ; or ”; and 
 (4)  by  adding  at the end the following: “(14)  the prevention, frustration, or resolution of the hostage taking of a United States person, the identification, location, arrest, or conviction of a person responsible for the hostage taking of a United States person, or the location of a United States person who has been taken hostage, in any country.”
