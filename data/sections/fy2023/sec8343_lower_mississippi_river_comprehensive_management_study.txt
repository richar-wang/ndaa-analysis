SEC. 8343.  LOWER MISSISSIPPI RIVER COMPREHENSIVE MANAGEMENT STUDY.   Section 213 of the Water Resources Development Act of 2020 ( 134 Stat. 2684 )  is amended  by  adding  at the end the following: “(j)  Cost Share .— The Federal share of the cost of the comprehensive study carried out under subsection (a), and any feasibility study carried out under subsection (e), shall be 100 percent.”
