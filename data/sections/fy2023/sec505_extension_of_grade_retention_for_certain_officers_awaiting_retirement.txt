SEC. 505.  EXTENSION OF GRADE RETENTION FOR CERTAIN OFFICERS AWAITING RETIREMENT.    Section 601(b)(5) of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended  by  striking  “ retirement, but not for more than 60 days. ” and  inserting  the following:  “retirement, but— “(A)  subject to subparagraph (B), not for more than 60 days; and 
 “(B)  with respect to an officer awaiting retirement following not less than one year of consecutive deployment outside of the United States to a combat zone (as defined in section 112(c) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986) or in support of a contingency operation, not for more than 90 days.”
