SEC. 10409.  Time period. AUTHORIZATION OF APPROPRIATIONS.   Section 409 of the Marine Mammal Protection Act of 1972 ( 16 U.S.C. 1421g )  is amended — (1)  in paragraph (1), by  striking  “ 1993 and 1994; ” and  inserting  “ 2023 through 2028; ”; 
 (2)  in paragraph (2), by  striking  “ 1993 and 1994; ” and  inserting  “ 2023 through 2028; ”; and 
 (3)  in paragraph (3), by  striking  “ fiscal year 1993. ” and  inserting  “ for each of fiscal years 2023 through 2028. ”.
