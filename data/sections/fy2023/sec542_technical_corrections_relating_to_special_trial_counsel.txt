SEC. 542.  TECHNICAL CORRECTIONS RELATING TO SPECIAL TRIAL COUNSEL. (a)  Technical Corrections .— Section 824a(c)(3) of title 10, United States Code  (article 24a(c)(3) of the Uniform Code of Military Justice),  is amended — (1)  by  striking  “ Subject to paragraph (4) ” and  inserting  “ Subject to paragraph (5) ”; and 
 (2)  in subparagraph (D), by  striking  “ an ordered rehearing ” and  inserting  “ an authorized rehearing ”. 
 
 (b)  10 USC 824a note . Effective Date .— The amendments made by subsection (a) shall take effect immediately after the coming into effect of the amendments made by section 531 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2022 ( Public Law 117–81 ;  135 Stat. 1692 ) as provided in section 539C of that Act ( 10 U.S.C. 801 note ). 136 STAT. 2582
