SEC. 5579.  22 USC 10261 . SANCTIONS EXCEPTION RELATING TO IMPORTATION OF GOODS. (a)  In General .— The authorities and requirements to impose sanctions under this subtitle shall not include the authority or requirement to impose sanctions on the importation of goods. 
 (b)  Good Defined .— In this section, the term “ good ” means any article, natural or man-made substance, material, supply, or manufactured product, including inspection and test equipment, and excluding technical data. 136 STAT. 3371
