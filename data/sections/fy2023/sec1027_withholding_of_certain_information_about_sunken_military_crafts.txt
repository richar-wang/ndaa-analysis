SEC. 1027.  WITHHOLDING OF CERTAIN INFORMATION ABOUT SUNKEN MILITARY CRAFTS.   Section 1406 of the Sunken Military Craft Act ( title XIV of Public Law 108–375 ;  10 U.S.C. 113 note )  is amended  by  adding  at the end the following new subsection: “(j)  Withholding of Certain Information .— Pursuant to subparagraphs (A)(ii) and (B) of section 552(b)(3) of title 5 United States Code, the Secretary concerned may withhold from public disclosure information and data about the location or related artifacts of a sunken military craft under the jurisdiction of the Secretary, if such disclosure would increase the risk of the unauthorized disturbance of one or more sunken military craft.”
