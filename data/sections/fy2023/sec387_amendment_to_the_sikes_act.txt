SEC. 387.  AMENDMENT TO THE SIKES ACT. (a)  Use of Natural Features .— Section 101(a)(3)(A) of the Sikes Act ( 16 U.S.C. 670a(a)(3)(A) )  is amended — (1)  by  redesignating  clauses (ii) and (iii) as clauses (iii) and (iv), respectively; and 
 (2)  by  inserting  after clause (i) the following: “(ii)  the use of natural and nature-based features to maintain or improve military installation resilience;” 
 . 
 
 (b)  Expanding and Making Permanent the Program for Invasive Species Management for Military Installations .— Section 101(g) of the Sikes Act ( 16 U.S.C. 670a(g) )  is amended — (1)  by  striking  the header and  inserting  “ Program for Invasive Species Management for Military Installations ”; and 
 (2)  in paragraph (1)— (A)  by  striking  “ During fiscal years 2009 through 2014, the ” and  inserting  “ The ”; and 
 (B)  by  striking  “ in Guam ”.
