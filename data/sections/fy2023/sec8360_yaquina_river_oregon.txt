SEC. 8360.  YAQUINA RIVER, OREGON.   The Secretary shall not require the non-Federal interest for the project for navigation, Yaquina River, Oregon, authorized by the first section of the Act of March 4, 1913 (chapter 144,  37 Stat. 819 ), to— (1)  provide a floating plant to the United States for use in maintaining the project; or 
 (2)  Determination. provide funds in an amount determined by the Secretary to be equivalent to the value of the floating plant as a non-Federal contribution to the cost of maintaining the project.
