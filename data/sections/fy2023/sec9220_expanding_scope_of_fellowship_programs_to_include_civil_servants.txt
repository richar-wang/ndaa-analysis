SEC. 9220.  EXPANDING SCOPE OF FELLOWSHIP PROGRAMS TO INCLUDE CIVIL SERVANTS. (a)  In General .— Section 47 of the State Department Basic Authorities Act of 1956 ( 22 U.S.C. 2719 )  is amended — (1)  in the first sentence— (A)  by  inserting  “ or the Civil Service ” after “ with the Foreign Service ”; and 
 (B)  by  striking  “ Foreign service Act of 1980 ” and  inserting  “ Foreign Service Act of 1980 ”; and 
 
 (2)  in the second sentence, by  inserting  “ or the Civil Service ” after “ Foreign Service ”. 
 
 (b)  Initial Report .— Not later than 30 days before expanding participation to include civil servants in any fellowship program of the Department, the Secretary shall submit a report to the appropriate congressional committees that— (1)  identifies the affected fellowship program; and 
 (2)  justifies expanding participation in such program. 
 
 (c)  Follow-up Report .— Not later than 1 year after the expansion of any fellowship program authorized under this section, the Secretary shall submit a follow-up report to the appropriate congressional committees that describes how the expansion of participation in such program has impacted the effectiveness of the program.
