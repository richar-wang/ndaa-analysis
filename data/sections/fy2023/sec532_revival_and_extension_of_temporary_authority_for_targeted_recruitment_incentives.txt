SEC. 532.  REVIVAL AND EXTENSION OF TEMPORARY AUTHORITY FOR TARGETED RECRUITMENT INCENTIVES.   Section 522(h) of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2016 ( Public Law 114–92 ;  10 U.S.C. 503 note ) is— (1)  is revived to read as it did immediately before its expiration on December 31, 2020; and 
 (2)  is amended — (A)  by  striking  the semicolon and  inserting  a comma; and 
 (B)  by  striking  “ 2020 ” and  inserting  “ 2025 ”.
