SEC. 413.  END STRENGTHS FOR MILITARY TECHNICIANS (DUAL STATUS). (a)  In General .— The minimum number of military technicians (dual status) as of the last day of fiscal year 2023 for the reserve components of the Army and the Air Force (notwithstanding  section 129 of title 10, United States Code ) shall be the following: (1)  For the Army National Guard of the United States, 22,294. 
 (2)  For the Army Reserve, 6,492. 
 (3)  For the Air National Guard of the United States, 10,994. 
 (4)  For the Air Force Reserve, 7,111. 
 
 (b)  Limitation on Number of Temporary Military Technicians (dual Status) .— The number of temporary military technicians (dual-status) employed under the authority of subsection (a) may not exceed 25 percent of the total authorized number specified in such subsection. 
 (c)  Limitation .— Under no circumstances may a military technician (dual status) employed under the authority of this section be coerced by a State into accepting an offer of realignment or conversion to any other military status, including as a member of the Active, Guard, and Reserve program of a reserve component.
