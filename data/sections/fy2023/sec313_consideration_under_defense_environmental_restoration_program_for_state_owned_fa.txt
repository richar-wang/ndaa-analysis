SEC. 313.  CONSIDERATION UNDER DEFENSE ENVIRONMENTAL RESTORATION PROGRAM FOR STATE-OWNED FACILITIES OF THE NATIONAL GUARD WITH PROVEN EXPOSURE OF HAZARDOUS SUBSTANCES AND WASTE. (a)  Definition of State-owned National Guard Facility .— Section 2700 of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended  by  adding  at the end the following new paragraph: “(4)  The term ‘ State-owned National Guard facility ’ includes land owned and operated by a State when such land is used for training the National Guard pursuant to chapter 5 of title 32 with funds provided by the Secretary of Defense or the Secretary of a military department, even though such land is not under the jurisdiction of the Department of Defense.” 
 . 136 STAT. 2503 
 (b)  Authority for Defense Environmental Restoration Program .— Section 2701(a)(1) of such title  is amended , in the first sentence, by  inserting  “ and at State-owned National Guard facilities ” before the period. 
 (c)  Responsibility for Response Actions .— Section 2701(c)(1) of such title  is amended  by  adding  at the end the following new subparagraph: “(D)  Each State-owned National Guard facility being used for training the National Guard pursuant to chapter 5 of title 32 with funds provided by the Secretary of Defense or the Secretary of a military department at the time of actions leading to contamination by hazardous substances or pollutants or contaminants.”
