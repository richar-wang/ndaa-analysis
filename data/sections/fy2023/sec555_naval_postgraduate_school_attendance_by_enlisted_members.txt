SEC. 555.  NAVAL POSTGRADUATE SCHOOL: ATTENDANCE BY ENLISTED MEMBERS. (a)  In General .— Subsection (a)(2)(D)(iii) of  section 8545 of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended  by  striking  “ only on a space-available basis ”. 
 (b)  Deadline. Briefing .— Six years after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Defense shall brief the Committees on Armed Services of the Senate and House of Representatives on the effects of increasing enrollment of enlisted members at the Naval Postgraduate School pursuant to the amendment made by subsection (a). Such briefing shall include the following elements: (1)  Any increase to the effectiveness, readiness, or lethality of the Armed Forces. 
 (2)  Effects on rates of recruitment, promotion (including compensation to members), and retention.
