SEC. 11270.  CARGO WAITING TIME REDUCTION.   Not Reports.  later than 90 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Commandant shall submit to the Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure of the House of Representatives and the Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation of the Senate a report that includes— (1)  an explanation of the extent to which vessels carrying cargo are complying with the requirements of  chapter 700 of title 46, United States Code ; 
 (2)  the status of the investigation on the cause of the oil spill that occurred in October 2021 on the waters over the San Pedro Shelf related to an anchor strike, including the expected date on which the Marine Casualty Investigation Report with respect to such spill will be released; and 
 (3)  with respect to such vessels, a summary of actions taken or planned to be taken by the Commandant to provide  136 STAT. 4065 
additional protections against oil spills or other hazardous discharges caused by anchor strikes.
