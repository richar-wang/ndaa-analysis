SECTION 1.  SHORT TITLE. (a)  In General .— This Act may be cited as the “ James M. Inhofe National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2023 ”. 
 (b)  10 USC 101 note . References .— Any reference in this or any other Act to the “National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2023” shall be deemed to be a reference to the “James M. Inhofe National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2023”.
