SEC. 11216.  GREAT LAKES BARGE INSPECTION EXEMPTION.    Section 3302(m) of title 46, United States Code ,  is amended — (1)  in the matter preceding paragraph (1) by  inserting  “ or a Great Lakes barge ” after “ seagoing barge ”; and 
 (2)  by  striking  “ section 3301(6) of this title ” and  inserting  “ paragraph (6) or (13) of section 3301 of this title ”.
