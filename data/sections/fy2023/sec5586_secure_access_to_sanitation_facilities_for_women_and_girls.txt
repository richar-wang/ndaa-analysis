SEC. 5586.  SECURE ACCESS TO SANITATION FACILITIES FOR WOMEN AND GIRLS.   Subsection (a) of section 501 of the Foreign Relations Authorization Act, Fiscal Years 1994 and 1995 ( 22 U.S.C. 2601 note )  is amended — (1)  by  redesignating  paragraphs (6) through (11) as paragraphs (7) through (12), respectively; and 
 (2)  by  inserting  after paragraph (5) the following: “(6)  the provision of safe and secure access to sanitation facilities, with a special emphasis on women and children;”
