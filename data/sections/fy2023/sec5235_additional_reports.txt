 “(2)  Report to president, congress, and the establishment .— Not later than 7 days after the date on which an Inspector General receives a report submitted under paragraph (1), the Inspector General shall submit to the President, the appropriate congressional committees, and the head of the establishment— “(A)  the report received under paragraph (1); and 
 “(B)  a report by the Inspector General containing any comments the Inspector General determines appropriate.”
