SEC. 8349.  ARGENTINE, EAST BOTTOMS, FAIRFAX-JERSEY CREEK, AND NORTH KANSAS LEVEES UNITS, MISSOURI RIVER AND TRIBUTARIES AT KANSAS CITIES, MISSOURI AND KANSAS. (a)  In General .— The project for flood control, Kansas Citys on Missouri and Kansas Rivers in Missouri and Kansas, authorized by section 5 of the Act of June 22, 1936 Flood Control Act of 1936 (chapter 688,  49 Stat. 1588 ;  58 Stat. 897 ;  121 Stat. 1054 ) is modified to direct the Secretary to— (1)  Determination. construct access manholes, or other features, in the Fairfax portion of such project to allow for regular inspection of project features if the Secretary determines that such work is— (A)  not required as a result of improper operation and maintenance of the project by the non­Federal interest; and 
 (B)  technically feasible and environmentally acceptable; and 
 
 (2)  Plan. plan, design, and carry out the construction described in paragraph (1) as a continuation of the construction of such project. 
 
 (b)  Cost Sharing .— The Federal share of the cost of planning, design, and construction of access manholes or other features under this section shall be 90 percent. 136 STAT. 3798
