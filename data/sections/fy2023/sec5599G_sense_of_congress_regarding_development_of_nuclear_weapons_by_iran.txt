SEC. 5599G.  SENSE OF CONGRESS REGARDING DEVELOPMENT OF NUCLEAR WEAPONS BY IRAN.   Congress— (1)  reiterates its commitment to ensuring Iran will never acquire a nuclear weapon; 
 (2)  supports the important work of the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) in safeguarding nuclear material around the globe; 
 (3)  condemns Iran for its lack of transparency and meaningful cooperation with the IAEA on the unresolved matter of uranium particles discovered at undeclared sites in Iran and additional escalatory actions related to its nuclear program; and 
 (4)  applauds the IAEA Board of Governors’ resolution urging Iran’s full cooperation with the IAEA on outstanding safeguards issues on an urgent basis.
