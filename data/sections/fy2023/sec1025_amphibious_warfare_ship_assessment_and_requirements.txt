SEC. 1025.  AMPHIBIOUS WARFARE SHIP ASSESSMENT AND REQUIREMENTS.    Section 8695 of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended  by  adding  at the end the following new subsection: “(e)  Amphibious Warfare Ships .— In preparing each assessment and requirement under subsection (a), the Commandant of the Marine Corps shall be specifically responsible for developing the requirements relating to amphibious warfare ships.”
