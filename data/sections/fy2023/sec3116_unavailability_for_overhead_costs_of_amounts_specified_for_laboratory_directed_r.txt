SEC. 3116.  UNAVAILABILITY FOR OVERHEAD COSTS OF AMOUNTS SPECIFIED FOR LABORATORY-DIRECTED RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT. (a)  In General .— Section 4812 of the Atomic Energy Defense Act ( 50 U.S.C. 2792 )  is amended  by  adding  at the end the following new subsection: “(c)  Limitation on Use of Funds for Overhead .— A national security laboratory may not use funds made available under section 4811(c) to cover the costs of general and administrative overhead for the laboratory.” 
 . 
 (b)  Repeal of Pilot Program .— Section 3119 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2017 ( Public Law 114–328 ;  50 U.S.C. 2791 note ) is  repealed .
