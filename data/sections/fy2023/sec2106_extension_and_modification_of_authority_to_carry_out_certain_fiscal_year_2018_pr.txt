 (b)  Modification of Authority to Carry Out Certain Fiscal Year 2018 Projects .— (1)  Kunsan air base, korea .— In the case of the authorization contained in the table in section 2101(b) of the Military Construction Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2018 ( division B of Public Law 115–91 ;  131 Stat. 1819 ) for Kunsan Air Base, Korea, for construction of an Unmanned Aerial Vehicle Hangar at the installation, the Secretary of the Army may— (A)  construct the hangar at Camp Humphries, Korea; and 
 (B)  remove primary scope associated with the relocation of the air defense artillery battalion facilities to include a ground based missile defense equipment area, fighting positions, a missile resupply area air defense artillery facility, a ready building and command post, a battery command post area, a safety shelter, and a guard booth. 
 
 (2)  Kwajalein atoll, hwajalein .— Section 2879(a)(1)(A) of the Military Construction Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2018 ( division B of Public Law 115–91 ;  131 Stat. 1874 )  is amended  by  striking  “ at least 26 family housing units ” and  inserting  “ not more than 26 family housing units ”. 136 STAT. 2975
