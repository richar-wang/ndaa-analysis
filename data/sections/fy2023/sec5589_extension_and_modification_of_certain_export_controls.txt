SEC. 5589.  EXTENSION AND MODIFICATION OF CERTAIN EXPORT CONTROLS. (a)  Extension of Export Prohibition on Munitions Items to the Hong Kong Police Force .— Section 3 of the Act entitled “An Act to prohibit the commercial export of covered munitions items to the Hong Kong Police Force”, approved November 27, 2019 ( Public Law 116–77 ;  133 Stat. 1173 ),  is amended  by  striking  “ shall expire ” and all that follows and  inserting  “ shall expire on December 31, 2024. ”. 
 (b)  Modification of Authority of President Under Export Control Reform Act of 2018 .— Section 1753(a)(2)(F) of the Export Control Reform Act of 2018 ( 50 U.S.C. 4812(a)(2)(F) )  is amended  by  inserting  “ , security, or ” before “ intelligence ”.
