SEC. 2811.  Deadlines. 10 USC 2802 note . REVISION OF UNIFIED FACILITIES GUIDE SPECIFICATIONS AND UNIFIED FACILITIES CRITERIA TO INCLUDE SPECIFICATIONS ON USE OF GAS INSULATED SWITCHGEAR AND CRITERIA AND SPECIFICATIONS ON MICROGRIDS AND MICROGRID CONVERTERS.  (a)  Gas Insulated Switchgear .— Not later than one year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Under Secretary of Defense for Acquisition and Sustainment shall modify the Unified Facilities Guide Specifications to include a distinct specification for medium voltage gas insulated switchgear. 
 (b)  Microgrids .— Not later than one year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Under Secretary of Defense for Acquisition and Sustainment shall— (1)  modify the Unified Facilities Criteria to include criteria for microgrids; and 
 (2)  modify the Unified Facilities Guide Specifications to include specifications for microgrids and microgrid controllers.
