SEC. 8348.  SENSE OF CONGRESS RELATING TO OKATIBBEE LAKE, MISSISSIPPI.   It is the sense of Congress that— (1)  there is significant shoreline sloughing and erosion at the Okatibbee Lake portion of the project for flood protection, Chunky Creek, Chickasawhay and Pascagoula Rivers, Mississippi, authorized by section 203 of the Flood Control Act of 1962 ( 76 Stat. 1183 ), which has the potential to impact infrastructure, damage property, and put lives at risk; and 
 (2)  addressing shoreline sloughing and erosion at a project of the Secretary, including at a location leased by non-Federal entities such as Okatibbee Lake, is an activity that is eligible to be carried out by the Secretary as part of the operation and maintenance of such project.
