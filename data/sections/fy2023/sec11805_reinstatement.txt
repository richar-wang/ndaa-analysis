SEC. 11805.  REINSTATEMENT. (a)  Reinstatement .— The text of section 12(a) of the Act of June 21, 1940 ( 33 U.S.C. 522(a) ), popularly known as the “Truman-Hobbs Act”, is— (1)  reinstated as it appeared on the day before the date of the enactment of section 8507(b) of the William M. (Mac) Thornberry National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2021 ( Public Law 116–283 ;  134 Stat. 4754 ); and 136 STAT. 4165 
 (2)  redesignated as the sole text of section 12 of the Act of June 21, 1940 ( 33 U.S.C. 522 ). 
 
 (b)  33 USC 522 note . Effective Date .— The provision reinstated under subsection (a) shall be treated as if such section 8507(b) had never taken effect. 
 (c)  Conforming Amendment .— The provision reinstated under subsection (a)  is amended  by  striking  “ , except to the extent provided in this section ”.
