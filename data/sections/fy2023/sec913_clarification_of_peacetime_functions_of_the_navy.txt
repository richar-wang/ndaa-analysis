SEC. 913.  CLARIFICATION OF PEACETIME FUNCTIONS OF THE NAVY.    Section 8062(a) of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended — (1)  in the second sentence, by  striking  “ primarily ” and  inserting  “ for the peacetime promotion of the national security interests and prosperity of the United States and ”; and 
 (2)  in the third sentence, by  striking  “ for the effective prosecution of war ” and  inserting  “ for the duties described in the preceding sentence ”.
