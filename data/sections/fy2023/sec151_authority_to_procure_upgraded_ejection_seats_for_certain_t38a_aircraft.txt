SEC. 151.  AUTHORITY TO PROCURE UPGRADED EJECTION SEATS FOR CERTAIN T–38A AIRCRAFT.   The Secretary of the Air Force is authorized to procure upgraded ejection seats for— (1)  all T–38A aircraft of the Air Force Global Strike Command that have not received an upgraded ejection seat under the T–38 Ejection Seat Upgrade Program; and 136 STAT. 2457 
 (2)  all T–38A aircraft of the Air Combat Command that have not received an upgraded ejection seat as part of such Program.
