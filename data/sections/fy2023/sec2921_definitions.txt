SEC. 2921.  DEFINITIONS.   In this part: (1)  Map .— The term “ Map ” means the map entitled “Lander County Selected Lands” and dated August 4, 2020. 
 (2)  Secretary concerned .— The term “ Secretary concerned ” means— 136 STAT. 3047 (A)  the Secretary, with respect to land under the jurisdiction of the Secretary; and 
 (B)  the Secretary of Agriculture, acting through the Chief of the Forest Service, with respect to National Forest System land.
