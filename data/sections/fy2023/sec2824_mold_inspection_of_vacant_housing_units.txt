SEC. 2824.  MOLD INSPECTION OF VACANT HOUSING UNITS.    Section 2891a of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended — (1)  by  redesignating  subsection (e) as subsection (f); and 
 (2)  by  inserting  after subsection (d) the following new subsection (e): “(e)  Requirements for Secretary Concerned .— The Secretary concerned shall be responsible for— “(1)  providing for a mold inspection of each vacant housing unit before any new tenant moves into the unit; and 
 “(2)  providing to the new tenant the results of the inspection.”
