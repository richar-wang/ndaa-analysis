SEC. 10101.  33 USC 3612 note . PURPOSE.   The purpose of this title is to promote and support— (1)  the monitoring, understanding, and exploration of the Great Lakes, oceans, bays, estuaries, and coasts; and 
 (2)  the collection, analysis, synthesis, and sharing of data related to the Great Lakes, oceans, bays, estuaries, and coasts to facilitate scientific research and operational decisionmaking.
