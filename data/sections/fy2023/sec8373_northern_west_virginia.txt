SEC. 8373.  NORTHERN WEST VIRGINIA. (a)  In General .— Section 571 of the Water Resources Development Act of 1999 ( 113 Stat. 371 ;  121 Stat. 1257 ;  134 Stat. 2719 )  is amended — (1)  in the section heading, by  striking  “ central ” and  inserting  “ northern ”; 
 (2)  by  striking  subsection (a) and  inserting  the following: “(a)  Definition of Northern West Virginia .— In this section, the term ‘ northern West Virginia ’ means the counties of Barbour, Berkeley, Brooke, Doddridge, Grant, Hampshire, Hancock, Hardy, Harrison, Jefferson, Lewis, Marion, Marshall, Mineral, Morgan, Monongalia, Ohio, Pleasants, Preston, Randolph, Ritchie, Taylor, Tucker, Tyler, Upshur, Wetzel, and Wood, West Virginia.” 
 ; 
 (3)  in subsection (b), by  striking  “ central ” and  inserting  “ northern ”; 
 (4)  in subsection (c), by  striking  “ central ” and  inserting  “ northern ”; and 
 (5)  in subsection (h), by  striking  “ $100,000,000 ” and  inserting  “ $120,000,000 ”. 
 
 (b)  Clerical Amendment .— The table of contents in section 1(b) of the Water Resources Development Act of 1999 ( 113 Stat. 269 )  is amended  by  striking  the item relating to section 571 and  inserting  the following: 
 
 “Sec. 571.  
 Northern West Virginia.”.
