SEC. 579C.  10 USC 1791 note . PUBLIC REPORTING ON CERTAIN MILITARY CHILD CARE PROGRAMS.    Deadline. Time period. Public information. Web posting. Not later than September 30, 2023, and each calendar quarter thereafter, the Secretary of Defense shall post, on a publicly accessible website of the Department of Defense, information regarding the Military Child Care in Your Neighborhood and Military Child Care in Your Neighborhood-Plus programs, disaggregated by State, ZIP code, and Armed Force. Such information shall include whether each such provider is nationally accredited or rated by the Quality Rating and Improvement System of the State.
