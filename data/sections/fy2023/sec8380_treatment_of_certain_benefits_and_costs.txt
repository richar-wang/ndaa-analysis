SEC. 8380.  TREATMENT OF CERTAIN BENEFITS AND COSTS.   Section 152(a) of the Water Resources Development Act of 2020 ( 33 U.S.C. 2213a(a) )  is amended  by  striking  “ a flood risk management project that incidentally generates seismic safety benefits in regions ” and  inserting  “ a flood risk management or coastal storm risk management project in a region ”.
