SEC. 1234.  EXTENSION AND MODIFICATION OF AUTHORITY TO PROVIDE ASSISTANCE TO COUNTER THE ISLAMIC STATE OF IRAQ AND SYRIA. (a)  In General .— Subsection (a) of section 1236 of the Carl Levin and Howard P. “Buck” McKeon National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2015 ( Public Law 113–291 ;  128 Stat. 3558 )  is amended , in the matter preceding paragraph (1), by  striking  “ December 31, 2022 ” and  inserting  “ December 31, 2023 ”. 
 (b)  Funding .— Subsection (g) of such section  is amended — (1)  by  striking  “ fiscal year 2022 ” and  inserting  “ fiscal year 2023 ”; and 
 (2)  by  striking  “ $345,000,000 ” and  inserting  “ $358,000,000 ”. 
 
 (c)  Limitation on Cost of Construction and Repair Projects .— Subsection (o)(5) of such section  is amended  by  striking  “ December 31, 2022 ” and  inserting  “ December 31, 2023 ”.
