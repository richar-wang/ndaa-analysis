SEC. 11413.  EXPANSION OF POSTGRADUATE OPPORTUNITIES FOR MEMBERS OF COAST GUARD IN MEDICAL AND RELATED FIELDS. (a)  14 USC 2770 note . In General .— The Commandant shall expand opportunities for members of the Coast Guard to secure postgraduate degrees in medical and related professional disciplines for the purpose of supporting Coast Guard clinics and operations. 
 (b)  Application of Law .— Individuals who receive assistance pursuant to subsection (a) shall be subject to the service obligations required under  section 2114 of title 10, United States Code . 
 (c)  Military Training Student Loads .— Section 4904(b)(3) of title 14, United States Code ,  is amended  by  striking  “ 350 ” and  inserting  “ 385 ”.
