SEC. 11801.  TERMS AND VACANCIES. (a)  In General .— Section 46101(b) of title 46, United States Code ,  is amended  by— (1)  in paragraph (2)— (A)  by  striking  “ one year ” and  inserting  “ 2 years ”; and 
 (B)  by  striking  “ 2 terms ” and  inserting  “ 3 terms ”; and 
 
 (2)  in paragraph (3)— (A)  by  striking  “ of the individual being succeeded ” and  inserting  “ to which such individual is appointed ”; 
 (B)  by  striking  “ 2 terms ” and  inserting  “ 3 terms ”; and 
 (C)  by  striking  “ the predecessor of that ” and  inserting  “ such ”. 
 
 
 (b)  46 USC 46101 note . Applicability .— The amendments made by this section shall not apply to Commissioners to whom section 403(b) of the Howard Coble Coast Guard and Maritime Transportation Act of 2014 ( Public Law 113–281 ) applies.
