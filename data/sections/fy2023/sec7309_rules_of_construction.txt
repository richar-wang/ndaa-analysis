SEC. 7309.  6 USC 825 . RULES OF CONSTRUCTION. (a)  Administrator .— Nothing in this subtitle shall be construed to supersede the civilian emergency management authority of the Administrator under the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act ( 42 U.S.C. 5121 et seq. ) or the Post Katrina Emergency Management Reform Act ( 6 U.S.C. 701 et seq. ). 
 (b)  Secretary .— Nothing in this subtitle shall be construed as providing new authority to the Secretary, except to coordinate and facilitate the development of the assessments and reports required pursuant to this subtitle.
