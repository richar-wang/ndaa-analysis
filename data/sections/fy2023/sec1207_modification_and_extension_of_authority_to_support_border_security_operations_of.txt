SEC. 1207.  MODIFICATION AND EXTENSION OF AUTHORITY TO SUPPORT BORDER SECURITY OPERATIONS OF CERTAIN FOREIGN COUNTRIES. (a)  Modification .— Subsection (e) of section 1226 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2016 ( Public Law 114–92 ;  129 Stat. 1056 ;  22 U.S.C. 2151 note )  is amended  by  striking  paragraph (4). 
 (b)  Extension .— Subsection (h) of such section  is amended  by  striking  “ December 31, 2023 ” and  inserting  “ December 31, 2025 ”.
