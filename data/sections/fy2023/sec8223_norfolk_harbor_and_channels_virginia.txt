SEC. 8223.  NORFOLK HARBOR AND CHANNELS, VIRGINIA.   Not later than December 31, 2023, the Secretary shall complete a post-authorization change report for the Anchorage F modifications to the project for navigation, Norfolk Harbor and Channels, Virginia, authorized by section 201 of the Water Resources Development Act of 1986 ( 100 Stat. 4090 ;  132 Stat. 3840 ).
