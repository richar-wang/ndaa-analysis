SEC. 8392.  ENHANCED DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM.   The Secretary shall fully implement opportunities for enhanced development at lakes located primarily in the State of Oklahoma under the authorities provided in section 3134 of the Water Resources Development Act of 2007 ( 121 Stat. 1142 ;  130 Stat. 1671 ) and section 164 of the Water Resources Development Act of 2020 ( 134 Stat. 2668 ).
