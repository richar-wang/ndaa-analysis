SEC. 8502.  NONPOINT SOURCE MANAGEMENT PROGRAMS.   Section 319(j) of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act ( 33 U.S.C. 1329(j) )  is amended  by  striking  “ subsections (h) and (i) not to exceed ” and all that follows through “ fiscal year 1991 ” and  inserting  “ subsections (h) and (i) $200,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2023 through 2027 ”.
