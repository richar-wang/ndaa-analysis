SEC. 1071.  ANNUAL REPORT ON PROCUREMENT OF EQUIPMENT BY STATE AND LOCAL GOVERNMENTS THROUGH THE DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE. (a)  In General .— The Secretary of Defense, in coordination with the Administrator of General Services, shall submit to the Committees on Armed Services of the Senate and House of Representatives an annual report that includes current information on the purchase of equipment under the procedures established under  section 281(a) of title 10, United States Code , and the recipients of such equipment. 
 (b)  Matters for Inclusion .— Each report under subsection (a) shall include the following for the year covered by the report: (1)  The catalog of equipment available for purchase under subsection (c) of  section 281 of title 10, United States Code . 
 (2)  For each purchase of equipment under the procedures established under subsection (a) of such section— (A)  the recipient State or unit of local government; 
 (B)  the type of equipment; 
 (C)  Cost. the cost of the equipment; and 
 (D)  the administrative costs under subsection (b) of such section. 
 
 (3)  Such other information the Secretary determines is necessary. 136 STAT. 2792 
 
 (c)  Termination .— The requirement to submit a report under subsection (a) shall terminate on the date that is five years after the date of the enactment of this Act.
