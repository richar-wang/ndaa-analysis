SEC. 11328.  SAFETY STANDARDS. (a)  In General .— Section 4502 of title 46, United States Code ,  is amended — (1)  in subsection (i)(4) by  striking  “ each of fiscal years 2018 through 2021 ” and  inserting  “ fiscal year 2023 ”; and 
 (2)  in subsection (j)(4) by  striking  “ each of fiscal years 2018 through 2021 ” and  inserting  “ fiscal year 2023 ”. 
 
 (b)  Authorization of Appropriations .— Section 9 of the Maritime Debris Act ( 33 U.S.C. 1958 )  is amended — (1)  in subsection (a) by  striking  “ each of fiscal years 2018 through 2022 ” and  inserting  “ fiscal year 2023 ”; and 
 (2)  in subsection (b) by  striking  “ 2702(1) ” and  inserting  “ 4902(1) ”.
