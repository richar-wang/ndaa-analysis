SEC. 5556.  SENSE OF CONGRESS.   It is the sense of Congress that— 136 STAT. 3344 (1)  the President should direct the United States Representative to the United Nations to use the voice, vote, and influence of the United States at the United Nations to advocate for more transparent assessments of countries by the International Narcotics Control Board; and 
 (2)  bilateral, plurilateral, and multilateral international cooperation is essential to combating the trafficking of covered synthetic drugs.
