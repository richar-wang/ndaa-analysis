SEC. 1422.  AUTHORIZATION OF APPROPRIATIONS FOR ARMED FORCES RETIREMENT HOME.   There is hereby authorized to be appropriated for fiscal year 2023 from the Armed Forces Retirement Home Trust Fund the sum of $152,360,000 of which— (1)  $75,360,000 is for operation, maintenance, construction and renovation; and 
 (2)  $77,000,000 is for major construction.
