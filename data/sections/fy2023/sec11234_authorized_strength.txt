SEC. 11234.  Determinations. AUTHORIZED STRENGTH.    Section 3702 of title 14, United States Code ,  is amended  by  adding  at the end the following: “(c)  The Secretary may vary the authorized end strength of the Coast Guard Selected Reserves for a fiscal year by a number equal to not more than 3 percent of such end strength upon a determination by the Secretary that varying such authorized end strength is in the national interest. 
 “(d)  The Commandant may increase the authorized end strength of the Coast Guard Selected Reserves by a number equal to not more than 2 percent of such authorized end strength upon a determination by the Commandant that such increase would enhance manning and readiness in essential units or in critical specialties or ratings.”
