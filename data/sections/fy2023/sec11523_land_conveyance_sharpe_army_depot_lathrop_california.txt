SEC. 11523.  Deadline. LAND CONVEYANCE, SHARPE ARMY DEPOT, LATHROP, CALIFORNIA.   Not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the Administrator of the Maritime Administration shall complete the land conveyance required under section 2833 of the William M. (Mac) Thornberry National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2021 ( Public Law 116–283 ).
