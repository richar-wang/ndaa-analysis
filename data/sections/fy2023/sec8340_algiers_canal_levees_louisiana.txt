SEC. 8340.  ALGIERS CANAL LEVEES, LOUISIANA. (a)  In General .— In accordance with section 328 of the Water Resources Development Act of 1999 ( 113 Stat. 304 ;  121 Stat. 1129 ), the Secretary shall resume operation, maintenance, repair, rehabilitation, and replacement of the Algiers Canal Levees, Louisiana, at full Federal expense. 
 (b)  Technical Amendment .— Section 328(c) of the Water Resources Development Act of 1999 ( 113 Stat. 304 ;  121 Stat. 1129 )  is amended  by  inserting  “ described in subsection (b) ” after “ the project ”.
