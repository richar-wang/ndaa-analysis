SEC. 8362.  CHARLESTON HARBOR, SOUTH CAROLINA.   The  Reimbursement. Secretary shall reimburse the non-Federal interest for project for navigation, Charleston Harbor, South Carolina, authorized by section 1401(1) of the Water Resources Development Act of 2016 ( 130 Stat. 1708 ), for advanced funds provided by the non-Federal interest for construction of the project that exceed the non-Federal share of the cost of construction of the project as soon as practicable after the completion of each individual contract for the project.
