SEC. 816.  MODIFICATION OF PROVISION RELATING TO DETERMINATION OF CERTAIN ACTIVITIES WITH UNUSUALLY HAZARDOUS RISKS.   Section 1684 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2022 ( Public Law 117–81 )  is amended — (1)  in subsection (a), 135 Stat. 2123 .  by  striking  “ 2022 and 2023 ” and  inserting  “ 2022 through 2024 ”; and 
 (2)  in subsection (b), 135 Stat. 2124 .  by  striking  “ September 30, 2023 ” and  inserting  “ September 30, 2024 ”.
