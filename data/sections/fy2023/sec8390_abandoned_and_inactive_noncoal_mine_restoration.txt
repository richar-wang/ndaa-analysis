SEC. 8390.  ABANDONED AND INACTIVE NONCOAL MINE RESTORATION.   Section 560 of the Water Resources Development Act of 1999 ( 33 U.S.C. 2336 )  is amended — (1)  in subsection (c), by  inserting  “ , on land held in trust by the Secretary of the Interior on behalf of, and for the benefit of, an Indian Tribe, or on restricted land of any Indian Tribe, ” after “ land owned by the United States ”; and 
 (2)  in subsection (e)— 136 STAT. 3832 (A)  by  striking  “ Rehabilitation ” and  inserting  “ Restoration ”; and 
 (B)  by  striking  “ Sacramento ” and  inserting  “ Albuquerque ”; and 
 
 (3)  in subsection (f), by  striking  “ $30,000,000 ” and  inserting  “ $50,000,000 ”.
