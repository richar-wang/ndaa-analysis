SEC. 8321.  MURRIETA CREEK, CALIFORNIA.    Section 103 of title I of appendix B of Public Law 106–377  ( 114 Stat. 1441A–65 ) (relating to the project for flood control, environmental restoration, and recreation, Murrieta Creek, California),  is amended — (1)  by  striking  “ $89,850,000 ” and  inserting  “ $277,194,000 ”; 
 (2)  by  striking  “ $57,735,000 ” and  inserting  “ $180,176,100 ”; and 
 (3)  by  striking  “ $32,115,000 ” and  inserting  “ $97,017,900 ”.
