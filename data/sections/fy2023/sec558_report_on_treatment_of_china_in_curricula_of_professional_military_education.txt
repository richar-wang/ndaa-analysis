SEC. 558.  REPORT ON TREATMENT OF CHINA IN CURRICULA OF PROFESSIONAL MILITARY EDUCATION. (a)  In General .— Not later than December 1, 2023, the Secretary of Defense shall submit to the Committees on Armed Services of the Senate and House of Representatives a report regarding the treatment of China in the curricula of institutions of military education, including changes to such treatment implemented in the five years preceding the date of such report. 
 (b)  Definitions .— In this section: (1)  The term “ institutions of military education ” means— (A)  the professional military education schools; 
 (B)  the senior level service schools; 
 (C)  the intermediate level service schools; 
 (D)  the joint intermediate level service school; 
 (E)  the Naval Postgraduate School; and 
 (F)  the Air Force Institute of Technology. 
 
 (2)  The terms “intermediate level service school”, “joint intermediate level service school”, and “senior level service school” have the meaning given such terms in  section 2151 of title 10, United States Code . 
 (3)  The term “ professional military education schools ” means the schools specified in  section 2162 of title 10, United States Code .
