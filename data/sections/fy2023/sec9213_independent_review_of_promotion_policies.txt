SEC. 9213.  INDEPENDENT REVIEW OF PROMOTION POLICIES.    Deadline. Not later than 18 months after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Comptroller General of the United States shall conduct a comprehensive review of the policies, personnel, organization, and processes related to promotions within the Department, including— (1)  a review of— (A)  the selection and oversight of Foreign Service promotion panels; and 
 (B)  the use of quantitative data and metrics in such panels; 
 
 (2)  Assessment. an assessment of the promotion practices of the Department, including how promotion processes are communicated to the workforce and appeals processes; and 
 (3)  Recommenda- tions. recommendations for improving promotion panels and promotion practices.
