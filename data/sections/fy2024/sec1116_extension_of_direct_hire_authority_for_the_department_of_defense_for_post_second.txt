SEC. 1116.  EXTENSION OF DIRECT HIRE AUTHORITY FOR THE DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE FOR POST-SECONDARY STUDENTS AND RECENT GRADUATES.   Section 1106(d) of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2017 ( 10 U.S.C. 1580 note  prec.)  is amended  by  striking  “ September 30, 2025 ” and  inserting  “ September 30, 2030 ”.
