SEC. 2807.  State listing.  AUTHORIZATION OF COST-PLUS INCENTIVE-FEE CONTRACTING FOR MILITARY CONSTRUCTION PROJECTS TO MITIGATE RISK TO THE SENTINEL PROGRAM SCHEDULE AND COST.   Notwithstanding  section 3323 of title 10, United States Code , the Secretary of Defense may authorize the use of contracts using cost-plus incentive-fee contracting for military construction projects associated with launch facilities, launch centers, and related infrastructure of the Sentinel intercontinental ballistic missile weapon system program of the Department of Defense for not more than one low-rate initial production lot at each of the following locations: (1)  F.E. Warren Air Force Base, Wyoming. 137 STAT. 746 
 (2)  Malmstrom Air Force Base, Montana. 
 (3)  Minot Air Force Base, North Dakota.
