SEC. 509D.  BRIEFING ON NUMBER OF GENERAL OFFICERS OF THE SPACE FORCE ON ACTIVE DUTY.   Not Deadline.  later than March 1, 2024, the Secretary of Defense shall submit to the Committees on Armed Services of the Senate and House of Representatives a briefing regarding the number of general officers of the Space Force on active duty. Such briefing shall include the following elements: (1)  Evaluation. The evaluation of the Secretary whether the current number of such general officers is sufficient to meet the requirements of— (A)  the Space Force; 
 (B)  joint duty assignments under  chapter 38 of title 10, United States Code ; and 
 (C)  the combatant commands. 
 
 (2)  Proposals. Any proposal of the Secretary to increase the maximum number (under section 526a of such title) of such general officers in order to meet such requirements in the future. 
 (3)  A justification for any such proposal.
