SEC. 6225.  INTERNET AT HARDSHIP POSTS.    Section 3 of the State Department Basic Authorities Act of 1956 ( 22 U.S.C. 2670 )  is amended — (1)  in subsection (l), by  striking  “ ; and ” and  inserting  a semicolon; 
 (2)  in subsection (m) by  striking  the period at the end and by  inserting  “ ; and ”; and 
 (3)  by  adding  at the end the following new subsection: “(n)  pay expenses to provide internet services in residences owned or leased by the United States Government in foreign countries for the use of Department personnel where Department personnel receive a post hardship differential equivalent to 30 percent or more above basic compensation.”
