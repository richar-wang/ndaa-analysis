SEC. 216.  MODIFICATION OF SUPPORT FOR RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT OF BIOINDUSTRIAL MANUFACTURING PROCESSES.   Section 215(c)(1) of the James M. Inhofe National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2023 ( Public Law 117–263 ;  10 U.S.C. 4841 note )  is amended  by  inserting  “ pharmaceutical biologics and associated precursor materials, ” after “ commodity chemicals, ”.
