SEC. 2888.  EXTENSION AND MODIFICATION OF ANNUAL UPDATES TO MASTER PLANS AND INVESTMENT STRATEGIES FOR ARMY AMMUNITION PLANTS.   Section 2834(d) of the Military Construction Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2022 ( division B of Public Law 117–81 ;  135 Stat. 2201 )  is amended — (1)  in the matter preceding paragraph (1), by  striking  “ March 31, 2026 ” and  inserting  “ March 31, 2030 ”; and 
 (2)  by  adding  at the end the following new paragraph: “(5)  A description of any changes to a master plan for an ammunition production facility made in response to global events, including pandemics and armed conflicts.”
