SEC. 1712.  COMPOSITION OF THE SPACE FORCE WITHOUT COMPONENT. (a)  Composition of the Space Force .— Section 9081(b) of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended — (1)  by  striking  paragraph (1); 
 (2)  by  redesignating  paragraphs (2) and (3) as paragraphs (1) and (2), respectively; and 
 (3)  in paragraph (1), as so redesignated, by  striking  “ , including ” and all that follows through “ emergency ”. 
 
 (b)  10 USC 9081 note . Effective Date .— The amendments made by subsection (a) shall take effect on the date of the certification by the Secretary of the Air Force under section 1745.
