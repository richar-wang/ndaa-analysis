SEC. 413.  END STRENGTHS FOR MILITARY TECHNICIANS (DUAL STATUS).   The minimum number of military technicians (dual status) as of the last day of fiscal year 2024 for the reserve components of the Army and the Air Force (notwithstanding  section 129 of title 10, United States Code ) shall be the following: (1)  For the Army National Guard of the United States, 22,294. 
 (2)  For the Army Reserve, 7,990. 
 (3)  For the Air National Guard of the United States, 10,994. 
 (4)  For the Air Force Reserve, 6,882.
