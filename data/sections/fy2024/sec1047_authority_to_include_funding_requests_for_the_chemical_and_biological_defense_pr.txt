SEC. 1047.  AUTHORITY TO INCLUDE FUNDING REQUESTS FOR THE CHEMICAL AND BIOLOGICAL DEFENSE PROGRAM IN BUDGET ACCOUNTS OF MILITARY DEPARTMENTS.   Section 1701(d)(2) of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 1994 ( Public Law 103–160 ;  50 U.S.C. 1522(d)(2) )  is amended  by  striking  “ may not be included in the budget accounts ” and  inserting  “ may be included in the budget accounts ”.
