SEC. 592.  DUE DATE FOR REPORT ON EFFORTS TO PREVENT AND RESPOND TO DEATHS BY SUICIDE IN THE NAVY.   Section 599A(c) of the James M. Inhofe National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2023 ( Public Law 117–263 )  is amended  by  striking  “ 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act ” and  inserting  “ September 30, 2024 ”.
