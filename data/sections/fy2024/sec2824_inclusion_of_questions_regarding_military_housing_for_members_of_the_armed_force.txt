SEC. 2824.  10 USC 2821 note . INCLUSION OF QUESTIONS REGARDING MILITARY HOUSING FOR MEMBERS OF THE ARMED FORCES IN STATUS OF FORCES SURVEY.    The Secretary of Defense shall include, at a minimum, in each status of forces survey of the Department of Defense conducted on or after the date of the enactment of this Act questions specifically relating to the following: (1)  Overall satisfaction with current military housing of members of the Armed Forces. 
 (2)  Satisfaction of such members with the physical condition of such military housing. 
 (3)  Satisfaction of such members with the affordability of such military housing. 
 (4)  Whether such military housing of such members has impacted any decision of such a member related to reenlistment in the Armed Forces.
