SEC. 6403.  ENHANCED OVERSIGHT OF THE OFFICE OF CIVIL RIGHTS. (a)  Report With Recommendations and Management Structure .— Not later than 270 days after the date of the enactment of this division, the Secretary shall submit to the appropriate congressional committees a report with any recommendations for the long-term structure and management of the Office of Civil Rights (OCR), including— 137 STAT. 997 (1)  Assessment. an assessment of the strengths and weaknesses of OCR’s investigative processes and procedures; 
 (2)  any changes made within OCR to its investigative processes to improve the integrity and thoroughness of its investigations; and 
 (3)  any recommendations to improve the management structure, investigative process, and oversight of the Office.
