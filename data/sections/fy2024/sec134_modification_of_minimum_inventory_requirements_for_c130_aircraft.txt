SEC. 134.  MODIFICATION OF MINIMUM INVENTORY REQUIREMENTS FOR C–130 AIRCRAFT. (a)  Minimum Inventory Requirement .— Section 146(a)(3)(B) of the James M. Inhofe National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2023 ( Public Law 117–263 ;  136 Stat. 2455 )  is amended  by  striking  “ 2023 ” and  inserting  “ 2024 ”. 
 (b)  Prohibition on Reduction of C–130 Aircraft Assigned to National Guard .— Section 146(b)(1) of the James M. Inhofe National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2023 ( Public Law 117–263 ;  136 Stat. 2455 )  is amended  by  striking  “ fiscal year 2023 ” and  inserting  “ fiscal years 2023 and 2024 ”.
