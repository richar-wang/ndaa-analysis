SEC. 1852.  EXTENSION OF CERTAIN DIRECT SPENDING REDUCTIONS.   Section 251A(6)(D) of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985 ( 2 U.S.C. 901a(6)(D) )  is amended — (1)  in clause (i), by  striking  “ 6 ” and  inserting  “ 7 ”; and 
 (2)  in clause (ii), by  striking  “ second 6 months ” and  inserting  “ last 5 months ”.
