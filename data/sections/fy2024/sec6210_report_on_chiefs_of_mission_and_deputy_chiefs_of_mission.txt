SEC. 6210.  REPORT ON CHIEFS OF MISSION AND DEPUTY CHIEFS OF MISSION.    Time period. Not later than April 1, 2024, and annually thereafter for the next 4 years, the Secretary shall submit to the appropriate congressional committees a report that includes— (1)  the Foreign Service cone of each current chief of mission and deputy chief of mission (or whoever is acting in the capacity of chief or deputy chief if neither is present) for each United States embassy at which there is a Foreign Service Officer filling either of those positions; and 
 (2)  Data. aggregated data for all chiefs of mission and deputy chiefs of mission described in paragraph (1), disaggregated by cone.
