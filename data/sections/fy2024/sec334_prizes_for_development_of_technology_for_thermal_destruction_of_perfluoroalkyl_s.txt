SEC. 334.  PRIZES FOR DEVELOPMENT OF TECHNOLOGY FOR THERMAL DESTRUCTION OF PERFLUOROALKYL SUBSTANCES OR POLYFLUOROALKYL SUBSTANCES. (a)  Prizes .— Section 330 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2021 ( Public Law 116–283 ;  10 U.S.C. 2661 note  prec.), as amended by section 343 of the James M. Inhofe National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2023 ( Public Law 117–263 ;  136 Stat. 2530 ), is further amended— (1)  in subsection (a), by  adding  at the end the following new paragraph: “(3)  Technology for the thermal destruction of perfluoroalkyl substances or polyfluoroalkyl substances.” 
 ; and 
 (2)  in subsection (g), by  striking  “ October 1, 2024 ” and  inserting  “ December 31, 2026 ”. 
 
 (b)  Authorization of Appropriations .— There is authorized to be appropriated to the Department of Defense for fiscal year 2024 $1,000,000 to carry out this section.
