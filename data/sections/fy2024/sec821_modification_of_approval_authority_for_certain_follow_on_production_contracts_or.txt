SEC. 821.  MODIFICATION OF APPROVAL AUTHORITY FOR CERTAIN FOLLOW-ON PRODUCTION CONTRACTS OR TRANSACTIONS.    Section 4022 of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended — (1)  in subsection (a)(2)(C)(i)(I), by  inserting  after “ subsection (d) ” the following: “ were met for the prior transaction for the prototype project that provided for the award of the follow-on production contract or transaction, and the requirements of subsection (f) ”; and 
 (2)  in subsection (d), by  adding  at the end the following new paragraph: 137 STAT. 327 
 “(3)  The requirements of this subsection do not apply to follow-on production contracts or transactions under subsection (f).”
