SEC. 2884.  MODIFICATION OF AUTHORITY OF SECRETARY OF THE ARMY TO ENTER INTO COOPERATIVE AGREEMENTS RELATING TO ACCESS AND MANAGEMENT OF AIR FORCE MEMORIAL.   Section 2863(e) of the Military Construction Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2002 ( division B of Public Law 107–107 ;  115 Stat. 1332 ), 40 USC 8903 note .   is amended  by  striking  “ the Foundation ” and  inserting  “ non-Federal Government entities, the Secretary of the Air Force, or both, ”.
