SEC. 2881.  INCREASE OF LIMITATION ON FEE FOR ARCHITECTURAL AND ENGINEERING SERVICES PROCURED BY MILITARY DEPARTMENTS. (a)  Army .— Section 7540(b) of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended  by  striking  “ 6 percent ” and  inserting  “ 10 percent ”. 
 (b)  Navy .— Section 8612(b) of such title  is amended  by  striking  “ 6 percent ” and  inserting  “ 10 percent ”. 
 (c)  Air Force .— Section 9540(b) of such title  is amended  by  striking  “ 6 percent ” and  inserting  “ 10 percent ”.
