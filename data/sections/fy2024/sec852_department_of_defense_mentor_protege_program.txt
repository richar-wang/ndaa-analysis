SEC. 852.  DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE MENTOR-PROTEGE PROGRAM.    Section 4902(e) of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended — (1)  in paragraph (1), by  redesignating  subparagraphs (A) through (D) as clauses (i) through (iv), respectively; 
 (2)  by  redesignating  paragraphs (1) and (2) as subparagraphs (A) and (B), respectively; 
 (3)  by  striking  “ Before providing assistance ” and  inserting  “ (1) Before providing assistance ”; and 
 (4)  by  adding  at the end the following new paragraph: “(2)  Contracts. An agreement under this subsection may be a contract, cooperative agreement, or a partnership intermediary agreement.”
