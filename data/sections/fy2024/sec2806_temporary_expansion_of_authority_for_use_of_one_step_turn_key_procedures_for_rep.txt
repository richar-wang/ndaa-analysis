SEC. 2806.  Time period. 10 USC 2862 note . TEMPORARY EXPANSION OF AUTHORITY FOR USE OF ONE-STEP TURN-KEY PROCEDURES FOR REPAIR PROJECTS.   During the five-year period beginning on the date of the enactment of this Act,  section 2862(a)(2) of title 10, United States Code , shall be applied and administered by substituting “$8,000,000” for “$4,000,000”.
