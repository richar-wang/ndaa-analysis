SEC. 702.  EXTENSION OF PERIOD OF ELIGIBILITY FOR HEALTH BENEFITS UNDER TRICARE RESERVE SELECT FOR SURVIVORS OF A MEMBER OF THE SELECTED RESERVE. (a)  In General .—   Section 1076d(c) of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended  by  striking  “ six months ” and  inserting  “ three years ”. 
 (b)  10 USC 1076d note . Effective Date .— The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect on October 1, 2025.
