SEC. 367.  10 USC 4811 note . RECOVERY OF RARE EARTH ELEMENTS AND OTHER STRATEGIC AND CRITICAL MATERIALS THROUGH END-OF-LIFE EQUIPMENT RECYCLING.   The Secretary of Defense shall issue policies and establish procedures to— (1)  identify end-of-life equipment of the Department of Defense that contains rare earth elements and other materials determined pursuant to section 3(a) of the Strategic and Critical Materials Stock Piling Act ( 50 U.S.C. 98b(a) ) to be strategic and critical materials; and Policies. Procedures. 
 (2)  recover such materials from such equipment for the purposes of reuse by the Department of Defense.
