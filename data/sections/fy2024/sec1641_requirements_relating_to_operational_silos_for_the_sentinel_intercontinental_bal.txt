SEC. 1641.  State listing. REQUIREMENTS RELATING TO OPERATIONAL SILOS FOR THE SENTINEL INTERCONTINENTAL BALLISTIC MISSILE.   The Secretary of the Air Force shall refurbish and make operable not fewer than 150 silos for the LGM–35A Sentinel intercontinental ballistic missile at each of the following locations: (1)  Francis E. Warren Air Force Base, Laramie County, Wyoming. 
 (2)  Malmstrom Air Force Base, Cascade County, Montana. 
 (3)  Minot Air Force Base, Ward County, North Dakota.
