SEC. 341.  MODIFICATION OF RULE OF CONSTRUCTION REGARDING PROVISION OF SUPPORT AND SERVICES TO NON-DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE ORGANIZATIONS AND ACTIVITIES.    Section 2012(i) of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended — (1)  by  redesignating  paragraphs (1) and (2) as subparagraphs (A) and (B), respectively; 
 (2)  in the matter preceding subparagraph (A), as redesignated by paragraph (1), by  striking  “ Nothing in this section ” and  inserting  “ (1) Nothing in this section ”; 
 (3)  in subparagraph (A), as so redesignated, by  inserting  “ , except as provided in paragraph (2), ” before “ for response ”; and 
 (4)  by  adding  at the end the following new paragraph: “(2)  Funds available to the Secretary of a military department for operation and maintenance for the Innovative Readiness Training program (as established pursuant to this section) may be expended under this section, upon approval by the Secretary concerned, to assist in demolition, clearing of roads, infrastructure improvements, and military construction to restore an area after a natural disaster.”
