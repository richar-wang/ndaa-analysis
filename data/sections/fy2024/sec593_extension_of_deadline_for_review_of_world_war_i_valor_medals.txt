SEC. 593.  EXTENSION OF DEADLINE FOR REVIEW OF WORLD WAR I VALOR MEDALS.   Section 584(f) of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2020 ( Public Law 116–92 ;  10 U.S.C. 7271 note )  is amended  by  striking  “ six years after the date of the enactment of this Act ” and  inserting  “ December 31, 2028 ”.
