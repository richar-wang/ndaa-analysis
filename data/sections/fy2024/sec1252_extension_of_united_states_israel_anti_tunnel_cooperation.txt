SEC. 1252.  EXTENSION OF UNITED STATES-ISRAEL ANTI-TUNNEL COOPERATION.   Section 1279(f) of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2016 ( Public Law 114–92 ;  129 Stat. 1079 ;  22 U.S.C. 8606 note )  is amended  by  striking  “ December 31, 2024 ” and  inserting  “ December 31, 2026 ”.
