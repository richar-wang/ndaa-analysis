SEC. 5501.  Beryllium Testing Fairness Act. AMENDMENTS TO THE ENERGY EMPLOYEES OCCUPATIONAL ILLNESS COMPENSATION PROGRAM ACT OF 2000. (a)  42 USC 7384 note . Short Title .— This section may be cited as the “ Beryllium Testing Fairness Act ”. 
 (b)  Modification of Demonstration of Beryllium Sensitivity .— Section 3621(8)(A) of the Energy Employees Occupational Illness Compensation Program Act of 2000 ( 42 U.S.C. 7384l(8)(A) )  is amended — (1)  by  striking  “ established by an abnormal ” and  inserting  the following:  “established by— “(i)  an abnormal” 
 ; 
 (2)  by  striking  the period at the end and  inserting  “ ; or ”; and 
 (3)  by  adding  at the end the following: “(ii)  three borderline beryllium lymphocyte proliferation tests performed on blood cells over a period of 3 years.” 
 . 
 
 (c)  Extension of Advisory Board on Toxic Substances and Worker Health .— Section 3687(j) of the Energy Employees Occupational Illness Compensation Program Act of 2000 ( 42 U.S.C. 7385s-16(j) )  is amended  by  striking  “ 10 years ” and  inserting  “ 15 years ”.
