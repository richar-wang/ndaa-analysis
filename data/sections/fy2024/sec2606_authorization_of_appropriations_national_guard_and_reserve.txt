SEC. 2606.  AUTHORIZATION OF APPROPRIATIONS, NATIONAL GUARD AND RESERVE.   Funds are hereby authorized to be appropriated for fiscal years beginning after September 30, 2023, for the costs of acquisition, architectural and engineering services, and construction of facilities for the Guard and Reserve Forces, and for contributions therefor, under  chapter 1803 of title 10, United States Code  (including the cost of acquisition of land for those facilities), as specified in the funding table in section 4601.
