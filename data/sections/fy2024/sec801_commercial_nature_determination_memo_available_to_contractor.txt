SEC. 801.  COMMERCIAL NATURE DETERMINATION MEMO AVAILABLE TO CONTRACTOR.    Section 3456(b)(2) of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended — (1)  by  striking  “ for such determination ” and  inserting  “ why the product or service was determined to be commercial or noncommercial ”; and 
 (2)  Records. by  adding  at the end the following: “ Upon the request of the contractor or subcontractor offering the product or service for which such determination is summarized in such memorandum, the contracting officer shall provide to such contractor or subcontractor a copy of such memorandum. ”.
