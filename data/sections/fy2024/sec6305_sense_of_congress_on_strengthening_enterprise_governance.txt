SEC. 6305.  SENSE OF CONGRESS ON STRENGTHENING ENTERPRISE GOVERNANCE.    It is the sense of Congress that in order to modernize the Department, enterprise-wide governance regarding budget and finance, information technology, and the creation, analysis, and use of data across the Department is necessary to better align resources to strategy, including evaluating trade-offs, and to enhance efficiency and security in using data and technology as  137 STAT. 989 
tools to inform and evaluate the conduct of United States foreign policy.
