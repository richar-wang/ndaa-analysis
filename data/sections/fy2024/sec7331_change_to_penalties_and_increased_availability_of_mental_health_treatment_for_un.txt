SEC. 7331.  CHANGE TO PENALTIES AND INCREASED AVAILABILITY OF MENTAL HEALTH TREATMENT FOR UNLAWFUL CONDUCT ON CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE AGENCY INSTALLATIONS.   Section 15(b) of the Central Intelligence Agency Act of 1949 ( 50 U.S.C. 3515(b) )  is amended , in the second sentence, by  striking  “ those specified in  section 1315(c)(2) of title 40, United States Code ” and  inserting  “ the maximum penalty authorized for a Class B misdemeanor under  section 3559 of title 18, United States Code ”.
