SEC. 1248.  REVIVAL OF AUTHORITY FOR PARTICIPATION OF NATO NAVAL PERSONNEL IN SUBMARINE SAFETY PROGRAMS. (a)  In General .— Subsection (e) of  section 8634 of title 10, United States Code , is  repealed . 
 (b)  Conforming Amendment .— Subsection (a) of such section 8634  is amended  by  striking  “ the Secretary of the Navy may conduct a program ” and  inserting  “ the Secretary of the Navy may conduct a program beginning on or after the date of the enactment of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2024 ”.
