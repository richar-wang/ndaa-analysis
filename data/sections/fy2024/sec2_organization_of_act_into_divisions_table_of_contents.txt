SEC. 2.  ORGANIZATION OF ACT INTO DIVISIONS; TABLE OF CONTENTS. (a)  Divisions .— This Act is organized into seven divisions as follows: (1)  Division A—Department of Defense Authorizations. 
 (2)  Division B—Military Construction Authorizations. 
 (3)  Division C—Department of Energy National Security Authorizations and Other Authorizations. 
 (4)  Division D—Funding Tables. 
 (5)  Division E—Other Matters. 
 (6)  Division F—Department of State Authorization Act of 2023. 
 (7)  Division G—Intelligence Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2024.
