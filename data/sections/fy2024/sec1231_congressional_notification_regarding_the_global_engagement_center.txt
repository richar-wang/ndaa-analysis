SEC. 1231.  Deadline. 22 USC 2656 . CONGRESSIONAL NOTIFICATION REGARDING THE GLOBAL ENGAGEMENT CENTER.   Not later than 30 days after making funds or personnel available to the Global Engagement Center established pursuant to section 1287 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2017 ( 22 U.S.C. 2656 note ), the Secretary of Defense shall provide to the congressional defense committees a notification that includes— (1)  an accounting of such funds or personnel; and 
 (2)  an explanation of the reason for the availability of such funds or personnel.
