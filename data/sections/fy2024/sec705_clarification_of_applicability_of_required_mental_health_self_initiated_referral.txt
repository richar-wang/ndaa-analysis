SEC. 705.  CLARIFICATION OF APPLICABILITY OF REQUIRED MENTAL HEALTH SELF-INITIATED REFERRAL PROCESS FOR MEMBERS OF THE SELECTED RESERVE.    Section 1090b(e) of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended — (1)  in paragraph (1), in the matter preceding subparagraph (A), by  inserting  “ described in paragraph (3) ” after “ member of the armed forces ”; and 
 (2)  by  adding  at the end the following new paragraph: “(3)  A member of the armed forces described in this paragraph is— 137 STAT. 301 “(A)  a member on active duty for a period of longer than 30 days; or 
 “(B)  a member of the Selected Reserve in a duty status.”
