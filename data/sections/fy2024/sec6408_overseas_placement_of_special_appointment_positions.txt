SEC. 6408.  Reports. OVERSEAS PLACEMENT OF SPECIAL APPOINTMENT POSITIONS.   Not later than 90 days after the date of the enactment of this division, the Secretary shall submit to the appropriate congressional committees a report on current special appointment positions at United States diplomatic missions that do not exercise significant authority, and all positions under schedule B or schedule C of  subpart C of part 213 of title 5, Code of Federal Regulations , at United States diplomatic missions. The report shall include the title and responsibilities of each position, the expected duration of the position, the name of the individual currently appointed to the position, and the hiring authority utilized to fill the position.
