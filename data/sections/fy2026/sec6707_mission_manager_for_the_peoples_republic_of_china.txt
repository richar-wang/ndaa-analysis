6707. Mission manager for the People’s Republic of China (a) Establishment of position (1) In general There shall be a mission manager for all intelligence collection matters relating to the People’s Republic of China. The mission manager shall be designated or appointed by the Director of National Intelligence. (2) Rule of construction Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the mission manager designated or appointed under paragraph (1) may be an individual serving in a position within the Office of the Director of National Intelligence. (b) Applicability The first mission manager under subsection (a) shall be designated or appointed not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act. (c) Termination This section shall terminate on December 31, 2030.
