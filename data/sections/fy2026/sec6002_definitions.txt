6002. Definitions In this division: (1) Congressional intelligence committees The term  congressional intelligence committees  has the meaning given such term in section 3 of the National Security Act of 1947 ( 50 U.S.C. 3003 ). (2) Intelligence community The term  intelligence community  has the meaning given such term in section 3 of the National Security Act of 1947 ( 50 U.S.C. 3003 ).
