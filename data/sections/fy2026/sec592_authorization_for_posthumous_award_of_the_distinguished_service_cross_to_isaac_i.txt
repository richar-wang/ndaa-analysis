592. Authorization for posthumous award of the distinguished-service cross to Isaac  Ike  Camacho for acts of valor in Vietnam (a) Waiver of time limitations Notwithstanding the time limitations specified in section 7274 of title 10, United States Code, or any other time limitation with respect to the awarding of certain medals to persons who served in the Armed Forces, the Secretary of the Army may award the distinguished-service cross under section 7272 of such title to Isaac  Ike  Camacho for the acts of valor in Vietnam described in subsection (b). (b) Acts of valor described The acts of valor referred to in subsection (a) are the actions of Isaac “Ike” Camacho from November 24, 1963, to July 13, 1965, in Vietnam for which he was previously awarded the Silver Star.
