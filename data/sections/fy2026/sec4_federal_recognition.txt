4. Federal recognition (a) In general Federal recognition is extended to the Lumbee Tribe of North Carolina. (b) Applicability of laws Except as otherwise provided in this Act, all Federal laws (including regulations) of general application to Indians and federally recognized Indian tribes, including the Act of June 18, 1934 (commonly known as the  Indian Reorganization Act ) (48 Stat. 984, chapter 576;  25 U.S.C. 5101 et seq. ), shall apply to the Lumbee Tribe of North Carolina and its members.
