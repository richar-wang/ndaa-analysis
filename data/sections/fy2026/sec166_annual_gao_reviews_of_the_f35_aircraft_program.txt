166. Annual GAO reviews of the F–35 aircraft program (a) Annual reviews and reports Not later than March 1, 2026, and not later than March 1 of each year thereafter through March 1, 2030, the Comptroller General of the United States shall— (1) complete a review of the F–35 aircraft program; and (2) submit to the congressional defense committees a report on the results of the review.  (b) Elements Each review and report under subsection (a) shall include an assessment of— (1) the cost, scope, and schedule of the F–35 aircraft program and its subprograms; (2) the status of the efforts of the Department of Defense to modernize the F–35 aircraft; and (3) such other matters relating to the F–35 aircraft program as the Comptroller General determines appropriate.
