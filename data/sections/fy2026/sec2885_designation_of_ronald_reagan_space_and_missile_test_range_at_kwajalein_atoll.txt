2885. Designation of Ronald Reagan Space and Missile Test Range at Kwajalein Atoll (a) Designation The site known as the  Ronald Reagan Ballistic Missile Defense Test Site  located at Kwajalein Atoll in the Republic of the Marshall Islands shall on and after the date of the enactment of this Act be known and designated as the  Ronald Reagan Space and Missile Test Range . (b) References Any reference in any law, regulation, map, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the site specified in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Ronald Reagan Space and Missile Test Range.  (c) Conforming repeal Section 2887 of the Military Construction Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2001 (division B of  Public Law 106–398 ; 114 Stat. 1654A–441) is repealed.
