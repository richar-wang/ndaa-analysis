1081. Extension of the National Commission on the Future of the Navy Section 1092 of the James M. Inhofe National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2023 ( Public Law 117–263 ; 136 Stat. 2809), as amended by section 1083 of the Service Member Quality of Life Improvement and National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2025 ( Public Law 118–159 ), is further amended— (1) in subsection (a)(4), by striking  January 15, 2026  and inserting  July 1, 2027 ; (2) in subsection (c)(3), by adding at the end the following new sentences:  The commission may request access to special access programs. The commission may employ personnel and obtain detailees who hold the security clearances necessary to review classified information. ; and (3) in subsection (e), by striking  90 days  and inserting  180 days .
