8201.  Authority of Marshal of the Supreme Court and Supreme Court Police Section 6121(a)(2) of title 40, United States Code, is amended by striking subparagraph (C) and inserting the following: (C) if the Marshal determines such protection is necessary— (i) any retired or former Chief Justice or Associate Justice of the Supreme Court; or (ii) any member of the immediate family of the Chief Justice, any Associate Justice, any retired or former Chief Justice or Associate Justice, or any officer of the Supreme Court. .
