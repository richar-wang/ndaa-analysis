146. Extension of prohibition on certain reductions to B–1 bomber aircraft squadrons Subsection (d)(1) of section 133 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2022 ( Public Law 117–81 ; 135 Stat. 1574), as most recently amended by section 146 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2025 ( Public Law 118–159 ; 138 Stat. 1810), is further amended by striking  September 30, 2026  and inserting  September 30, 2030 .
