862. Repeal of limitations on certain Department of Defense Executive Agent authority Section 1792 of the John S. McCain National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2019 ( Public Law 115–232 ; 132 Stat. 2238;  50 U.S.C. 4531  note) and section 226 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2018 ( Public Law 115–91 ;  50 U.S.C. 4531  note) are repealed.
