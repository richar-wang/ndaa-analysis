1083. Provision of contract authority to Afghanistan War Commission Subsection (f) of the Afghanistan War Commission Act of 2021 (section 1094(f) of  Public Law 117–81 ; 135 Stat. 1941) is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph: (6) Contract authority To such extent and in such amounts as are provided in appropriation Acts, the Co-Chairpersons of the Commission may enter into contracts to enable the Commission to discharge its duties under this section. .
