623. Extending certain travel allowance for members of the Armed Forces assigned to Alaska Section 603(b) of the James M. Inhofe National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2023 ( Public Law 117–263 ) is amended— (1) in paragraph (2), by amending subparagraph (A) to read as follows: (A) the member is— (i) assigned to a duty location in Alaska for a period of not less than one year; and (ii) unaccompanied by dependents in such duty location; and ; and (2) by amending paragraph (5) to read as follows: (5) Period specified The period specified in this paragraph is the period— (A) beginning on the date of the enactment of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2026; and (B) ending on December 31, 2027. .
