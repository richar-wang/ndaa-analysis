8753. Maximum contingent liability Section 1433 of the Better Utilization of Investments Leading to Development Act of 2018 ( 22 U.S.C. 9633 ) is amended to read as follows: 1433. Maximum contingent liability (a) In general The maximum contingent liability of the Corporation outstanding at any one time shall not exceed in the aggregate $205,000,000,000. (b) Rule of construction The maximum contingent liability shall apply to all extension of liability by the Corporation regardless of the authority cited thereto. .
