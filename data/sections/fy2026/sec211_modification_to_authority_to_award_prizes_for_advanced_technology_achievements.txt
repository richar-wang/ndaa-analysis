211. Modification to authority to award prizes for advanced technology achievements (a) Authority Subsection (a) of section 4025 of title 10, United States Code, is amended by inserting after  the Under Secretary of Defense for Acquisition and Sustainment,  the following:  the Director of the Defense Innovation Unit, . (b) Maximum amount of award prizes Subsection (c) of such section is amended— (1) in paragraph (1) by striking  $10,000,000  and inserting  $20,000,000 ; (2) in paragraph (2) by striking  $1,000,000  and inserting  $2,000,000 ; and (3) in paragraph (3) by striking  $10,000  and inserting  $20,000 . (c) Congressional notification threshold Subsection (g)(1) of such section is amended by striking  $10,000,000  and inserting  $20,000,000 .
