3114. Protection of certain nuclear facilities and assets from unmanned aircraft Section 4510(e)(1)(C) of the Atomic Energy Defense Act ( 50 U.S.C. 2661(e)(1)(C) ) is amended to read as follows: (C) (i) owned by or contracted to the National Nuclear Security Administration, including any facility that stores or uses special nuclear material; or  (ii) a national security laboratory or nuclear weapons production facility. .
