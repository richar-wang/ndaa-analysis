Inclusion of additional landlord financial information in certain annual report on privatized military housing Section 2891c(a)(2) of title 10, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraphs: (G) Information with respect to each insurance policy maintained by the landlord for such housing units, including the— (i) scope of coverage; (ii) deductible; (iii) policy limit; and (iv) total premium amount. (H) The total amount of any payments made by the landlord to tenants of such housing units pursuant to a dispute resolution process. .
