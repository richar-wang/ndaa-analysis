553. Asynchronous instruction in distance education option for professional military education Subsection (c)(1) of section 2154 of title 10, United States Code, as added by section 555 of the Servicemember Quality of Life Improvement and National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2025 ( Public Law 118–159 ; 138 Stat. 1896), is amended by inserting  asynchronously and  after  course of instruction .
