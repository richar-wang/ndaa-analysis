2863. Extension of prohibition on joint use of Homestead Air Reserve Base with civil aviation Section 2874 of the Military Construction Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2023 (division B of  Public Law 117–263 ; 136 Stat. 3014), as amended by section 2808 of the Military Construction Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2025 (division B of  Public Law 118–159 ), is further amended by striking  September 30, 2028  and inserting  September 30, 2034 .
