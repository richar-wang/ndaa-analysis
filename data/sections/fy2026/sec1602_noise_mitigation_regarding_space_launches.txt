1602. Noise mitigation regarding space launches Section 2276a of title 10, United States Code, is amended— (1) by redesignating subsections (c) and (d) as subsections (d) and (e), respectively; and (2) by inserting after subsection (b) the following new subsection: (c) Noise mitigation at Space Force launch sites In providing space launch support services to a commercial entity, the Secretary of the Air Force shall coordinate with the entity to— (1) study the noise caused by launches at launch sites of the Space Force; and (2) identify potential technologies and procedures to mitigate such noise to the extent practicable. .
