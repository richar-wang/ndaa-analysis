Improvements to requirement for monitoring of Iranian enrichment of uranium-235 Section 7413(b) of the Intelligence Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2024 (division G of  Public Law 118–31 ;  22 U.S.C. 8701  note) is amended— (1) by redesignating paragraph (2) as paragraph (3); (2) in paragraph (1), by striking  assesses that the Islamic Republic of Iran has produced or possesses any amount of uranium-235 enriched to greater than 60 percent purity or has engaged in significant enrichment activity,  and inserting  makes a finding described in paragraph (2) pursuant to an assessment, ; and (3) by inserting after paragraph (1) the following: (2) Finding described A finding described in this paragraph is a finding that the Islamic Republic of Iran has— (A) produced or possesses any amount of uranium-235 enriched to greater than 60-percent purity; (B) engaged in significant enrichment activity; or (C) made the decision to produce a nuclear weapon from highly enriched uranium. .
