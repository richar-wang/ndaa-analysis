2608. Modification of authority to carry out fiscal year 2023 project at Tucson International Airport, Arizona In the case of the authorization contained in the table in section 2604 of the Military Construction Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2023 (division B of  Public Law 117–263 ; 136 Stat. 2987) for Tucson International Airport, Arizona, the Secretary of the Air Force may acquire 10 acres of land.
