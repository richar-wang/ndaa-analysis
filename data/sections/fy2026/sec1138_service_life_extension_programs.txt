1138. Service life extension programs (a) In general Requirements for a Level 1 or Level 2 acquisition project or program under sections 1131 through 1134 shall not apply to an acquisition by the Coast Guard that is a service life extension program. (b) Service life extension program defined In this section, the term  service life extension program  means a capital investment that is solely intended to extend the service life and address obsolescence of components or systems of a particular capability or asset.
