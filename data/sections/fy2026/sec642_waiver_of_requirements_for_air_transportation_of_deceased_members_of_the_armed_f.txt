642. Waiver of requirements for air transportation of deceased members of the Armed Forces when necessary to meet mission requirements Section 562(c) of the John Warner National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2007 ( Public Law 109–364 ;  10 U.S.C. 1482  note) is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:  (4) Waiver The Secretary concerned may waive the requirements of paragraphs (1) and (3) as the Secretary considers necessary to meet mission requirements during— (A) a time of war;  (B) a national emergency requiring the use of significant personnel and aircraft; (C) a large-scale combat operation; or (D) a contingency operation. .
