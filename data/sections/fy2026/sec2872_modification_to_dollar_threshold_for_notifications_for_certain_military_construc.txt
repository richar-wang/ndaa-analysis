2872. Modification to dollar threshold for notifications for certain military construction projects (a) Notification for certain unspecified minor military construction projects Section 2805(b)(2) of title 10, United States Code, as amended by section 2871, is further amended by striking  $4,000,000  and inserting  $6,000,000 . (b) Notification for certain architectural and engineering services and construction design Section 2807(b) of title 10, United States Code, is amended by striking  $1,000,000  and inserting  $5,000,000 .
