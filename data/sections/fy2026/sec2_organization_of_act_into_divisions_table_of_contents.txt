(2) Division B—Military Construction Authorizations. (3) Division C—Department of Energy National Security Authorizations and Other Authorizations. (4) Division D—Funding Tables. (5) Division E—Department of State Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2026. (6) Division F—Intelligence Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2026. (7) Division G—Coast Guard Authorization Act of 2025.
