5405. Repeal of limitation on use of funds for international expositions Section 204 of the Admiral James W. Nance and Meg Donovan Foreign Relations Authorization Act, Fiscal Years 2000 and 2001 ( 22 U.S.C. 2452b ) (as enacted into law by section 1000(a)(7) of the  Public Law 106–113  and contained in appendix G of that Act; 113 Stat. 1501A–486)) is repealed.
