Chief Executive Officer Section 1413(d)(3) of the Better Utilization of Investments Leading to Development Act of 2018 ( 22 U.S.C. 9613(d)(3) ) is amended to read as follows: (3) Relationship to board The Chief Executive Officer shall— (A) report to and be under the direct authority of the Board; and (B) take input from the Board when assessing the performance of the Chief Risk Officer, established pursuant to subsection (f), the Chief Development Officer, established pursuant to subsection (g), and the Chief Strategic Officer, established pursuant to subsection (h). .
