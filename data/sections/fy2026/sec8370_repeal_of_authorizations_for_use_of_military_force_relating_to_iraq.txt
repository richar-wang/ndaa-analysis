8370. Repeal of Authorizations for Use of Military Force relating to Iraq The following are hereby repealed: (1) Authorization for Use of Military Force Against Iraq Resolution ( Public Law 102–1 ; 105 Stat. 3;  50 U.S.C. 1541  note) is hereby repealed. (2) The Authorization for Use of Military Force Against Iraq Resolution of 2002 ( Public Law 107–243 ; 116 Stat. 1498;  50 U.S.C. 1541  note) is hereby repealed.
