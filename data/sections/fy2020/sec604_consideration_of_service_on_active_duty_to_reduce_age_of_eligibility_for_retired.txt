SEC. 604.  CONSIDERATION OF SERVICE ON ACTIVE DUTY TO REDUCE AGE OF ELIGIBILITY FOR RETIRED PAY FOR NON-REGULAR SERVICE.    Section 12731(f)(2)(B)(i) of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended  by  striking  “ under a provision of law referred to in section 101(a)(13)(B) or under section 12301(d) ” and  inserting  “ under section 12301(d) or 12304b of this title, or under a provision of law referred to in section 101(a)(13)(B) ”.
