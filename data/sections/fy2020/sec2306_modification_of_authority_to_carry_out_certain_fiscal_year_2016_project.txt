SEC. 2306.  Nevada. MODIFICATION OF AUTHORITY TO CARRY OUT CERTAIN FISCAL YEAR 2016 PROJECT.   The table in section 2301(a) of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2016 ( Public Law 114–92 ;  129 Stat. 1152 )  is amended  in the item relating to Nellis Air Force Base, Nevada, by  striking  “ $68,950,000 ” and  inserting  “ $72,050,000 ” for construction of F–35A Munitions Maintenance Facilities, as specified in the funding table in section 4601 of such Act ( 129 Stat. 1293 ).
