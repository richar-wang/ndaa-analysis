SEC. 314.  DISPOSAL OF RECYCLABLE MATERIALS.    Section 2577(a) of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended  by  adding  at the end the following new paragraph: “(3)  Definition. In this section, the term ‘ recyclable materials ’ may include any quality recyclable material provided to the Department by a State or local government entity, if such material is authorized  133 STAT. 1304 
by the Office of the Secretary of Defense and identified in the regulations prescribed under paragraph (1).”
