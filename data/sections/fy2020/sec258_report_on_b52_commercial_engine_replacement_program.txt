SEC. 258.  REPORT ON B–52 COMMERCIAL ENGINE REPLACEMENT PROGRAM. (a)  Documentation Required .— The Secretary of the Air Force shall submit to the congressional defense committees a report on the B–52 commercial engine replacement program of the Air Force. 
 (b)  Strategies. Contents .— The report submitted under subsection (a) shall include the following: (1)  The acquisition strategy of the Secretary for the program. 
 (2)  Estimates. The cost and schedule estimates of the Secretary for the program. 
 (3)  The key performance parameters or equivalent requirements document for the program. 
 (4)  The test and evaluation strategy of the Secretary for the program. 
 (5)  The logistics strategy of the Secretary for the program. 
 (6)  The post-production fielding strategy of the Secretary for the program. 
 (7)  Assessment. An assessment of the potential for the commercial engine replacement to achieve nuclear system certification. 
 
 (c)  Limitation .— Of the funds authorized to be appropriated by this Act or otherwise made available for fiscal year 2020 for the Air Force, not more than 75 percent may be obligated or expended until the date on which the Secretary of the Air Force  133 STAT. 1293 
submits to the congressional defense committees the report required by subsection (a).
