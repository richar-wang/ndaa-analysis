SEC. 707.  10 USC 1074m note . PROVISION OF BLOOD TESTING FOR FIREFIGHTERS OF DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE TO DETERMINE EXPOSURE TO PERFLUOROALKYL AND POLYFLUOROALKYL SUBSTANCES. (a)  Effective date. In General .— Beginning on October 1, 2020, the Secretary of Defense shall provide blood testing to determine and document potential exposure to perfluoroalkyl and polyfluoroalkyl substances (commonly known as “PFAS”) for each firefighter of the Department of Defense during the annual physical exam conducted by the Department for each such firefighter. 
 (b)  Firefighter Defined .— In this section, the term “ firefighter ” means someone whose primary job or military occupational specialty is being a firefighter.
