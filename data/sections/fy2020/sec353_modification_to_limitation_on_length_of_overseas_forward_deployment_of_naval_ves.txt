SEC. 353.  MODIFICATION TO LIMITATION ON LENGTH OF OVERSEAS FORWARD DEPLOYMENT OF NAVAL VESSELS.   Section 323 of the John S. McCain National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2019 ( Public Law 115–232 ) 10 USC 8690 note .   is amended — (1)  by  redesignating  subsection (c) as subsection (d); and 
 (2)  by  inserting  after subsection (b) the following new subsection (c): “(c)  Extension of Limitation on Length of Overseas Forward Deployment for U.S.S. Shiloh (CG– 67 ) .— Deadline. Notwithstanding subsection (b), the Secretary of the Navy shall ensure that the U.S.S. Shiloh (CG–67) is assigned a homeport in the United States by not later than September 30, 2023.”
