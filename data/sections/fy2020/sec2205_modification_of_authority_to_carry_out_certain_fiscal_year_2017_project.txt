SEC. 2205.  MODIFICATION OF AUTHORITY TO CARRY OUT CERTAIN FISCAL YEAR 2017 PROJECT.   The table in section 2201(a) of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2017 ( Public Law 114–328 ;  130 Stat. 2691 )  is amended  in the item relating to Bangor, Washington, by  striking  “ $113,415,000 ” and  inserting  “ $161,415,000 ” for construction of a SEAWOLF Class Service Pier, as specified in the funding table in section 4601 of such Act ( 130 Stat. 2876 ).
