SEC. 1012.  MODIFICATION OF REQUIRED ELEMENTS OF ANNUAL REPORTS ON EMERGENCY AND EXTRAORDINARY EXPENSES OF THE DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE.   Paragraph (2) of  section 127(d) of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended  to read as follows: “(2)  Each report submitted under paragraph (1) shall include, for each individual expenditure covered by such report in an amount in excess of $100,000, the following: “(A)  A detailed description of the purpose of such expenditure. 
 “(B)  The amount of such expenditure. 
 “(C)  An identification of the approving authority for such expenditure. 
 “(D)  A justification why other authorities available to the Department could not be used for such expenditure. 
 “(E)  Any other matters the Secretary considers appropriate.”
