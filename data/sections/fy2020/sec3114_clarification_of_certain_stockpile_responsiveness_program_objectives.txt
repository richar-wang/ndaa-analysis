SEC. 3114.  CLARIFICATION OF CERTAIN STOCKPILE RESPONSIVENESS PROGRAM OBJECTIVES.   Section 4220(c) of the Atomic Energy Defense Act ( 50 U.S.C. 2538b(c) )  is amended — (1)  in paragraph (3), by  striking  “ capabilities required, including prototypes ” and  inserting  “ capabilities as required, such as through the use of prototypes ”; and 
 (2)  in paragraph (6)— (A)  by  striking  “ in consultation with the Director of National Intelligence ” and  inserting  “ in coordination with the Director of National Intelligence ”; and 
 (B)  by  inserting  “ if needed to meet intelligence requirements ” after “ foreign countries ”.
