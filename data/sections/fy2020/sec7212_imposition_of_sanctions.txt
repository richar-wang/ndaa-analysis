SEC. 7212.  President. 21 USC 2312 . IMPOSITION OF SANCTIONS.   The President shall impose five or more of the sanctions described in section 7213 with respect to each foreign person that is an entity, and four or more of such sanctions with respect to each foreign person that is an individual, that— (1)  is identified as a foreign opioid trafficker in a report submitted under section 7211(a); or 
 (2)  Determination. the President determines is owned, controlled, directed by, knowingly supplying or sourcing precursors for, or knowingly acting for or on behalf of, such a foreign opioid trafficker. 133 STAT. 2266
