SEC. 608.  REDUCTIONS ON ACCOUNT OF EARNINGS FROM WORK PERFORMED WHILE ENTITLED TO AN ANNUITY SUPPLEMENT.    Section 8421a(c) of title 5, United States Code ,  is amended — (1)  by  striking  “ full-time as an air traffic control instructor ” and  inserting  “ as an air traffic control instructor, or supervisor thereof, ”; and 
 (2)  by  inserting  “ or supervisor ” after “ an instructor ”.
