SEC. 6401.  AUTHORITY FOR PROTECTION OF CURRENT AND FORMER EMPLOYEES OF THE OFFICE OF THE DIRECTOR OF NATIONAL INTELLIGENCE.   Section 5(a)(4) of the Central Intelligence Agency Act of 1949 ( 50 U.S.C. 3506(a)(4) )  is amended  by  striking  “ such personnel of the Office of the Director of National Intelligence as the Director of National Intelligence may designate; ” and  inserting  “ current and former personnel of the Office of the Director of National  133 STAT. 2196 
Intelligence and their immediate families as the Director of National Intelligence may designate; ”.
