SEC. 7235.  21 USC 2335 . EXCEPTION RELATING TO IMPORTATION OF GOODS. (a)  In General .— The authorities and requirements to impose sanctions under this title shall not include the authority or a requirement to impose sanctions on the importation of goods. 
 (b)  Good Defined .— In this section, the term “ good ” means any article, natural or manmade substance, material, supply, or manufactured product, including inspection and test equipment, and excluding technical data.
