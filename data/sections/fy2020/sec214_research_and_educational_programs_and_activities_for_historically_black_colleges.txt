SEC. 214.  RESEARCH AND EDUCATIONAL PROGRAMS AND ACTIVITIES FOR HISTORICALLY BLACK COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES AND MINORITY-SERVING INSTITUTIONS OF HIGHER EDUCATION.    Section 2362 of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended — (1)  by  redesignating  subsections (d) and (e) as subsections (e) and (f), respectively; and 
 (2)  by  inserting  after subsection (c) the following new subsection: “(d)  Incentives .— The Secretary of Defense may develop incentives to encourage research and educational collaborations between covered educational institutions and other institutions of higher education.”
