SEC. 722.  10 USC 1071 note . MODIFICATION TO REFERRALS FOR MENTAL HEALTH SERVICES.   If the Secretary Deadline.  of Defense is unable to provide mental health services in a military medical treatment facility to a member of the Armed Forces within 15 days of the date on which such services are first requested by the member, the Secretary may refer the member to a provider under the TRICARE program (as that term is defined in  section 1072 of title 10, United States Code ) to receive such services.
