SEC. 530B.  SENSE OF CONGRESS REGARDING ACCESSION PHYSICALS.   It is the sense of Congress that the Secretary of Defense should explore alternatives to centralized accession physicals at Military Entrance Processing Stations, including conducting physicals through community health care providers, in order to reduce  133 STAT. 1359 
transportation costs, increase efficiency in processing times, and free recruiters to focus on the core of the recruiting mission.
