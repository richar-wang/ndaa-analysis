SEC. 1107.  EXTENSION OF DIRECT HIRE AUTHORITY FOR DOMESTIC INDUSTRIAL BASE FACILITIES AND MAJOR RANGE AND TEST FACILITIES BASE. (a)  In General .— Subsection (a) of section 1125 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2017 ( Public Law 114–328 ), as amended by subsection (a) of section 1102 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2018 ( Public Law 115–91 ), 10 USC 1580 note  prec.  is further amended by  striking  “ through 2021, ” and  inserting  “ through 2025, ”. 
 (b)  Briefing .— Subsection (b) of such section 1102  is amended  by  striking  “ fiscal years 2019 and 2021 ” and  inserting  “ fiscal years 2019 through 2025 ”.
