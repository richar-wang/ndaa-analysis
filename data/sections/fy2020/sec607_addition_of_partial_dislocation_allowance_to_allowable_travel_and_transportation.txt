SEC. 607.  ADDITION OF PARTIAL DISLOCATION ALLOWANCE TO ALLOWABLE TRAVEL AND TRANSPORTATION EXPENSES FOR SERVICEMEMBERS. (a)  Current Authority .— Section 477(f)(1) of title 37, United States Code ,  is amended  by  striking  “ family ”. 
 (b)  Future Authority .— Section 452(c) of title 37, United States Code ,  is amended — (1)  by  redesignating  paragraph (3) as paragraph (4); and 
 (2)  by  inserting  after paragraph (2) the following new paragraph (3): “(3)  A partial dislocation allowance paid to a member ordered to occupy or vacate housing provided by the United States.”
