SEC. 2915.  AUTHORIZATION OF EMERGENCY SUPPLEMENTAL APPROPRIATIONS FOR MILITARY CONSTRUCTION PROJECTS.   Funds are hereby authorized to be appropriated for the Department of Defense for the military construction projects authorized by this subtitle as specified in the funding table in section 4603, in such amounts as may be designated as emergency requirements pursuant to section 251(b)(2)(A)(i) of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985 ( 2 U.S.C. 901(b)(2)(A)(i) ). 133 STAT. 1915
