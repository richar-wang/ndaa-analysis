SEC. 6414.  REPEAL OF FOREIGN LANGUAGE PROFICIENCY REQUIREMENT FOR CERTAIN SENIOR LEVEL POSITIONS IN THE CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE AGENCY. (a)  Repeal of Foreign Language Proficiency Requirement .— Section 104A of the National Security Act of 1947 ( 50 U.S.C. 3036 )  is amended  by  striking  subsection (g). 
 (b)  Conforming Repeal of Report Requirement .— Section 611 of the Intelligence Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2005 ( Public Law 108–487 ) 118 Stat. 3955 .   is amended  by  striking  subsection (c).
