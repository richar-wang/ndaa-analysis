SEC. 623.  DEATH GRATUITY FOR ROTC GRADUATES. (a)  In General .— Section 1475(a)(4) of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended  by  adding  “ ; or a graduate of a reserve officers’ training corps who has received a commission but has yet to receive a first duty assignment; or ” at the end. 
 (b)  10 USC 1475 note . Effective Date .— The amendment under subsection (a) applies to deaths that occur on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
