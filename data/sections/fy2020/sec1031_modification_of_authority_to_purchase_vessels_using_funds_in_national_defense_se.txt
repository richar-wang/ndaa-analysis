SEC. 1031.  MODIFICATION OF AUTHORITY TO PURCHASE VESSELS USING FUNDS IN NATIONAL DEFENSE SEALIFT FUND. (a)  In General .— Section 2218(f)(3)(E) of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended — (1)  in clause (i), by  striking  “ ten new sealift vessels ” and  inserting  “ ten new vessels that are sealift vessels, auxiliary vessels, or a combination of such vessels ”; and 
 (2)  in clause (ii), by  striking  “ sealift ”. 
 
 (b)  10 USC 2218 note . Effective Date .— The amendments made by subsection (a) shall take effect on October 1, 2019, and shall apply with respect to fiscal years beginning on or after that date.
