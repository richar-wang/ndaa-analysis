SEC. 7433.  President. IMPLEMENTATION AND REGULATORY AUTHORITIES. (a)  Implementation Authority .— The President may exercise all authorities provided to the President under sections 203 and 205 of the International Emergency Economic Powers Act ( 50 U.S.C. 1702  and 1704) for purposes of carrying out this title and the amendments made by this title. 
 (b)  Deadline. Regulatory Authority .— The President shall, not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, promulgate regulations as necessary for the implementation of this title and the amendments made by this title.
