SEC. 3132.  REPEAL OF ASSESSMENTS OF ADEQUACY OF BUDGET REQUESTS RELATING TO NUCLEAR WEAPONS STOCKPILE. (a)  In General .— Section 3255 of the National Nuclear Security Administration Act ( 50 U.S.C. 2455 ) is  repealed . 
 (b)  Clerical Amendment .— The table of contents for the National Nuclear Security Administration Act  is amended  by  striking  the item relating to section 3255.
