SEC. 1024.  ASSESSMENT OF IMPACT OF ANY PLANNED OR PROPOSED BORDER WALL ON VOLUME OF ILLEGAL NARCOTICS. (a)  Consultation. Assessment Required .— The Secretary of Defense, in consultation with the Secretary of Homeland Security, shall conduct an assessment of the impact that any planned or proposed border wall construction under section 284 or 2808 of  title 10, United States Code , along the southern border of the United States would have on the volume of illegal narcotics entering the United States. 
 (b)  Report .— Not later than 90 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Defense and the Secretary of Homeland Security shall jointly submit to Congress a report on the assessment required by subsection (a).
