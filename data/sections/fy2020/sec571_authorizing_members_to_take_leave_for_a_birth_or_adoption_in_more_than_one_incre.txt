SEC. 571.  AUTHORIZING MEMBERS TO TAKE LEAVE FOR A BIRTH OR ADOPTION IN MORE THAN ONE INCREMENT.   Paragraph (5) of  section 701(i) of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended — (1)  by  striking  “ only in one increment ” and  inserting  “ in more than one increment ”; and 
 (2)  by  inserting  “ in accordance with regulations prescribed by the Secretary concerned ” before the period.
