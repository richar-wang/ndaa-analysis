SEC. 6314.  Deadlines. 50 USC 3024 note . POLICY ON MINIMUM INSIDER THREAT STANDARDS. (a)  Policy Required .— Not later than 60 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Director of National Intelligence shall establish a policy for minimum insider threat standards that is consistent with the National Insider Threat Policy and Minimum Standards for Executive Branch Insider Threat Programs. 
 (b)  Implementation .— Not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the head of each element of the intelligence community shall implement the policy established under subsection (a).
