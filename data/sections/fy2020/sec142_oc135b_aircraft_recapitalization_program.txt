SEC. 142.  OC–135B AIRCRAFT RECAPITALIZATION PROGRAM.   The Secretary of the Air Force shall ensure that any request for proposals for the procurement of an OC–135B aircraft under a recapitalization program for such aircraft meets the requirements for full and open competition as set forth in  section 2304 of title 10, United States Code , and includes, as part of such request for proposals, consideration of proposals for the provision of new production aircraft and recently manufactured aircraft.
