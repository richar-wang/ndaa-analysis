SEC. 1704.  EXTENSION OF REQUIREMENT FOR BRIEFINGS ON THE NATIONAL BIODEFENSE STRATEGY.   Section 1086(d) of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal year 2017 ( Public Law 114–328 ;  130 Stat. 2423 ;  6 U.S.C. 104(d) )  is amended  by  striking  “ March 1, 2019 ” and  inserting  “ March 1, 2025 ”.
