SEC. 3018.  ACCESS BY TENANTS OF PRIVATIZED MILITARY HOUSING TO MAINTENANCE WORK ORDER SYSTEM.    Section 2892 of title 10, United States Code , as added by section 3017,  is amended  by  adding  at the end the following new subsection: “(c)  Requirement. Access by Tenants .— The Secretary of Defense shall require each landlord of a housing unit to provide access to the maintenance work order system of the landlord relating to the  133 STAT. 1931 
housing unit to the tenant of the housing unit to permit the tenant, at a minimum, to track the status and progress of work orders for maintenance requests relating to the housing unit.”
