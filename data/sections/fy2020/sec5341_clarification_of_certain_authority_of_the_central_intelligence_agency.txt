SEC. 5341.  Determination. CLARIFICATION OF CERTAIN AUTHORITY OF THE CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE AGENCY.   Section 8(a)(1) of the Central Intelligence Agency Act of 1949 ( 50 U.S.C. 3510(a)(1) )  is amended  by  inserting  before “ rental of ” the following: “ payment of death benefits in cases in which the circumstances of the death of an employee of the Agency, a detailee of the Agency or other employee of another department or agency of the Federal Government assigned to the Agency, or an individual affiliated with the Agency (as determined by the Director), is not covered by section 11, other similar provisions of Federal law, or any regulation issued by the Director providing death benefits, but that the Director determines such payment appropriate; ”.
