SEC. 2303.  IMPROVEMENTS TO MILITARY FAMILY HOUSING UNITS.   Subject to  section 2825 of title 10, United States Code , and using amounts appropriated pursuant to the authorization of appropriations in section 2304(a) and available for military family housing functions as specified in the funding table in section 4601, the Secretary of the Air Force may improve existing military family housing units in an amount not to exceed $53,584,000.
