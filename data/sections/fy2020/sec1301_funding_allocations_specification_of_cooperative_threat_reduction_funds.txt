SECTION 1301.  FUNDING ALLOCATIONS; SPECIFICATION OF COOPERATIVE THREAT REDUCTION FUNDS. (a)  Funding Allocations .— Of the $338,700,000 authorized to be appropriated to the Department of Defense for fiscal year 2010 in section 301 and made available by the funding table in division D for the Department of Defense Cooperative Threat Reduction Program established under section 1321 of the Department of Defense Cooperative Threat Reduction Act ( 50 U.S.C. 3711 ), the following amounts may be obligated for the purposes specified: (1)  For strategic offensive arms elimination, $492,000. 
 (2)  For chemical weapons destruction, $12,856,000. 
 (3)  For global nuclear security, $33,919,000. 
 (4)  For cooperative biological engagement, $183,642,000. 
 (5)  For proliferation prevention, $79,869,000. 
 (6)  For activities designated as Other Assessments/Administrative Costs, $27,922,000. 
 
 (b)  Specification of Cooperative Threat Reduction Funds .— Funds appropriated pursuant to the authorization of appropriations in section 301 and made available by the funding table in division D for the Department of Defense Cooperative Threat Reduction Program shall be available for obligation for fiscal years 2020, 2021, and 2022.
