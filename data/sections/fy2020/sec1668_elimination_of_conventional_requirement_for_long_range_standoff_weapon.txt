SEC. 1668.  ELIMINATION OF CONVENTIONAL REQUIREMENT FOR LONG-RANGE STANDOFF WEAPON.   Subsection (a) of section 217 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2014 ( Public Law 113–66 ;  127 Stat. 706 ), as amended by section 1662 of the John S. McCain National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2019 ( Public Law 115–232 ;  132 Stat. 2152 ),  is amended  to read as follows: “(a)  Long-range Standoff Weapon .— The Secretary of the Air Force shall develop a follow-on air-launched cruise missile to the AGM–86 that— “(1)  achieves initial operating capability for nuclear missions prior to the retirement of the nuclear-armed AGM–86; and 
 “(2)  is capable of internal carriage and employment for nuclear missions on the next-generation long-range strike bomber.”
