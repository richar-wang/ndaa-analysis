SEC. 561.  REQUIREMENT TO PROVIDE INFORMATION REGARDING BENEFITS CLAIMS TO MEMBERS DURING TAP COUNSELING.    Section 1142(b) of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended  by  adding  at the end the following new paragraph: “(19)  Information regarding how to file claims for benefits available to the member under laws administered by the Secretaries of Defense and Veterans Affairs.”
