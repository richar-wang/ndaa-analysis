SEC. 3031.  INSTALLATION OF CARBON MONOXIDE DETECTORS IN MILITARY FAMILY HOUSING.    Section 2821 of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended  by  adding  at the end the following new subsection: “(e)  The Secretary concerned shall provide for the installation and maintenance of an appropriate number of carbon monoxide detectors in each unit of military family housing under the jurisdiction of the Secretary.”
