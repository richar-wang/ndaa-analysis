SEC. 1209.  TWO-YEAR EXTENSION OF PROGRAM AUTHORITY FOR GLOBAL SECURITY CONTINGENCY FUND.   Section 1207 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2012 ( 22 U.S.C. 2151 note )  is amended — (1)  in subsection (i)— (A)  in paragraph (1), by  striking  “ September 30, 2019 ” and  inserting  “ September 30, 2021 ”; and 
 (B)  by  amending  paragraph (2) to read as follows: “(2)  Exception .— Amounts appropriated and transferred to the Fund before September 30, 2019, shall remain available for obligation and expenditure after that date, but only for activities under programs commenced under subsection (b) before September 30, 2019.”

 (2)  in subsection (o)— (A)  in the first sentence, by  striking  “ September 30, 2019 ” and  inserting  “ September 30, 2021 ”; and
