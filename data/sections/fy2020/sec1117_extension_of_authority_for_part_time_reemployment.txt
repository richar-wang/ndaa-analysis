SEC. 1117.  EXTENSION OF AUTHORITY FOR PART-TIME REEMPLOYMENT. (a)  Civil Service Retirement System .— Section 8344(l)(7) of title 5, United States Code ,  is amended  by  striking  “ December 31, 2019 ” and  inserting  “ December 31, 2024 ”. 
 (b)  Federal Employees Retirement System .— Section 8468(i)(7) of title 5, United States Code ,  is amended  by  striking  “ December 31, 2019 ” and  inserting  “ December 31, 2024 ”.
