SEC. 1211.  EXTENSION OF AUTHORITY TO TRANSFER DEFENSE ARTICLES AND PROVIDE DEFENSE SERVICES TO THE MILITARY AND SECURITY FORCES OF AFGHANISTAN. (a)  Extension of Authority .— Subsection (h) of section 1222 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2013 ( Public Law 112–239 ;  126 Stat. 1992 )  is amended  by  striking  “ December 31, 2020 ” and  inserting  “ December 31, 2022 ”. 
 (b)  Excess Defense Articles .— Subsection (i)(2) of such section  is amended  by  striking  “ December 31, 2020 ” each place it appears and  inserting  “ December 31, 2022 ”.
