SEC. 6432.  NOTICE NOT REQUIRED FOR PRIVATE ENTITIES.    Section 3553 of title 44, United States Code ,  is amended — (1)  by  redesignating  subsection (j) as subsection (k); and 
 (2)  by  inserting  after subsection (i) the following: “(j)  Rule of Construction .— Nothing in this section shall be construed to require the Secretary to provide notice to any private entity before the Secretary issues a binding operational directive under subsection (b)(2).”
