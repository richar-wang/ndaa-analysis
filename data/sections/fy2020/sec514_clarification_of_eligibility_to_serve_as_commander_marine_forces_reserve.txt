SEC. 514.  CLARIFICATION OF ELIGIBILITY TO SERVE AS COMMANDER, MARINE FORCES RESERVE. (a)  In General .— Section 8084(b)(1) of title 10, United States Code ,  is amended  by  striking  “ general officers of the Marine Corps (as defined in section 8001(2)) ” and  inserting  “ general officers of the Marine Corps Reserve ”. 
 (b)  10 USC 8084 note . Effective Date .— The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect on the date that is one year after the date of the enactment of this Act and shall apply to appointments made after such date.
