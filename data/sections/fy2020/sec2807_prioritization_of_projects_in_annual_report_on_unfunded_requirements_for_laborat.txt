SEC. 2807.  PRIORITIZATION OF PROJECTS IN ANNUAL REPORT ON UNFUNDED REQUIREMENTS FOR LABORATORY MILITARY CONSTRUCTION PROJECTS.   Section 2806 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2018 ( Public Law 115–91 ;  10 U.S.C. 222a note )  is amended — (1)  by  striking  “ Assistant Secretary of Defense for Energy, Installations, and Environment ” and  inserting  “ Under Secretary of Defense for Acquisition and Sustainment ”; 
 (2)  by  striking  “ reporting ” and  inserting  “ report ”; and 
 (3)  by  inserting  “ in prioritized order, with specific accounts and program elements identified, ” after “ evaluation facilities, ”.
